Theise gentlewoman be exceed fat , and the lesser their nose be , the fair be they esteem : they daub over their sweet face with grease too shameful : and they never lie in bed for their travel of childbirth . of the duty enjoin unto the tartarian woman , and of their labour , and also of their marriage .
