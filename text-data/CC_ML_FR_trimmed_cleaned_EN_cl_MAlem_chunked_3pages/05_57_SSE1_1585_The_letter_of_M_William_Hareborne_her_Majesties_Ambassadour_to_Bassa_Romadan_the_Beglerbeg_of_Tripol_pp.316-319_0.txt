a letter of master William hareborne , the english ambassador , Ligier in Constantinople , to the bassa Romadan , the Beglerbeg of Tripoli in Barbary , for the restore of a english ship call the Jesus , with the good , and man , detain as slave , anno 1585 - 317 [ ii i . 194 . ] right honourable lord , it have be signify unto we by diverse letter , what have fall out , concern a certain ship of we , call the Jesus , into which , for the help of Richard skeg , one of our merchant in the same , now decease , there be admit a certain Frenchman call roman sonning , which for his ill behaviour , accord to his desert , seek to carry away with he another Frenchman , which be indebt to certain of your people , without pay his cteditour , be hang by sentence of justice , together with Andrew Dier , the master of the say ship , who simple and without fraud , give credit to the say Frenchman , without any knowledge of his evil fact , do not return when he be command , by your honourable lordship . the death of the say lewd Frenchman we approve as a thing well do , but contrariwise , whereas your lordship have confiscate the say ship with the good therein , and have make slave of the mariner , as a thing altogether contrary to the privilege of the grand signior , grant four year since , and confirm by we on the behalf of the most excellent the queen majesty of England our mistress , and altogether contrary to the league of the say grand signior , who be full inform of the aforesaid cause , have grant unto we his royal commandment of restitution , which we send unto your honourable lordship , by the present bearer Edward Barton our secretary , and Mahomet beg , one of the justice of his stately court , with other letter of the most excellent admiral , and most valiant captain of the sea , require your honourable lordship , as well on the behalf of the grand signior , as of the queen most excellent majesty my mistress , that the man , oil , ship , furniture , money , and all other good whatsoever , by your lordship , and your order take from our man , be restore unto this my secretary free , without delay , as the grand signior of his goodness have grant 318 unto we , especial in regard that the same oil be buy by the commandment of our queen most excellent majesty , for the provision of her court . which if you perform not , we protest by these our letter against you , that you be the cause of all the inconvenience which may ensue upon this occasion , as the author thereof , contrary to the holy league swear by both our prince , as by the privilege , which this our servant will show you , may appear . for the see of which league perform , we remain here as Ligier in this stately court . and by this mean you shall answer in another world unto God alone , and in this world unto the grand signior , for this heinous sin commit by you against so many poor soul , which by this your cruelty be in part dead , and in part detain by you in most miserable captivity . contrariwise , if it shall please you to avoid this mischief , and to remain in the favour of almighty God , and of our prince , you shall friendly fulfil this our just demand ( as it behoove you to show your self a prudent governor , and faitbfull servant unto your lord ) and the same may turn to your great honour , and profit , by the trade of merchandise , which our man in time to come , may use in that government of you : which general , as well those poor man , as all other , which you shall meet at the sea , aught to be accord to the commandment of the grand signior , friendly entertain and receive of your honourable lordship , and we will not fade in the duty of a speciali friend , whensoever you shall have occasion to use we , as we desire . Almighty God grant unto your lordship ( in the fulfil of this our just request , whereby we may be deliver from further trouble in this matter , and your self from further displeasure ) all true felicity , and increase of honour . give in our palace from Rapamat in Pera , the 15 . of Januarie 1585 .
