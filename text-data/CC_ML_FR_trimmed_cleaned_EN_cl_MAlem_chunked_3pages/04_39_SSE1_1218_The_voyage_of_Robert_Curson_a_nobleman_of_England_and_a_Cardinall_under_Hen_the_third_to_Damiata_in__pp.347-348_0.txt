the travail of Robert Curson . the same in english . RObert Curson descend of a noble family of England , use great diligence aswell in profane as in divine study in the famous university of Oxford ( as i conjecture . ) he have there the best schoolmaster that be to be get , and be most industrious in the art and continual exercise of learning : by mean whereof he grow to be of great renown where he live . afterwards think of great matter he go to Paris , and thence to roam it self , and at parish he proceed doctor of divinity , at room he be make cardinali : whereupon both Matthew Paris and Matthew of Westminster produce this testimony of he , the one in his second book , the other in his eight book of chronicle . in the year of our lord ( say they ) 1218 , at the take of Damiata a city of Egypt under John burn king of Jerusalem , m . Robert Curson a english man , a most famous clerk of noble parentage , and cardinali of the church of room , be there with Pelagius Albanensis , etc Boston of bury in Suffolk in his catalogue report , that he write diverse book . he flourish in the year aforesaid by the witness aforesaid . Henry the three son of king John be then king of England : and by the further testimony of Boston , this Curson be legate into England in the day of honorious the three , bishop of Rome .
