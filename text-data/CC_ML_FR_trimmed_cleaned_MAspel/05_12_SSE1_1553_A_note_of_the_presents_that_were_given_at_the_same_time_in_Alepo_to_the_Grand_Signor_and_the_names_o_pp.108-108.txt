A note of the presents that were given at the same time in Aleppo , to the grand Signior , and the names of the presenters . 
 First the Basham of Aleppo , which is as a Viceroy , presented 100. garments of cloth of gold , and 25. horses . 
 The Basham of Damasco , presented 100. garments of cloth of gold , and twentie horses , with diverse sorts of comfits , in great quantity . 
 The Basham of Aman presented 100. garments of cloth of gold , 20. horses , and a cup of gold , with two thousand ducats . 
 The Saniaque of Tripolis presented six camels , charged all with silks , 20. horses , and a little clock of gold , garnished with precious stones , esteemed worth two hundred ducats . 
 [ II i . 114. ] The Consul of the company of the Venetians in 
 Tripolis , came to kiss the grand Signiors hand , and presented him a great basin of gold , and therein 4000. ducats Venetians .
