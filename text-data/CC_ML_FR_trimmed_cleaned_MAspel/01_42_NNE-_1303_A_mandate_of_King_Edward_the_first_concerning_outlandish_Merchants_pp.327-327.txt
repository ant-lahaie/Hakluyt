Mandatum regis Edwardi primi de mercatoribus alienigenis . 
 MErcatores extranei vendant mercimonia sua in civitate London etc. infra quadraginta dies post ingressum suum , anno 3. Edwardi primi . 
 The same in English . 
 A mandate of king Edward the first concerning outlandish merchants . 
 WE will and command that outlandish merchants do sei their wares in the city of London etc. within forty days of their arrival .
