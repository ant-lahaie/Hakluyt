And be benight thou shall go westnorthwest , or west and by north until day : and be day then thou may hale in with sight of the land Southwest , because thou may be sure to come right in with it . if thou go from cape deal Aguja for Cartagena , if it be by day , thou shall go west and by south , and shall go to have sight of Morro Hermoso , that be the fair mountain , which lie to the westward of Rio grande .
