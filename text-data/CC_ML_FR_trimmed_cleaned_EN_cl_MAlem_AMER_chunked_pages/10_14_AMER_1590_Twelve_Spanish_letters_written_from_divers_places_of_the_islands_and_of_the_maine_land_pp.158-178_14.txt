Indians , Mullatos , and mestizo , call the small pock , and a certain plague , which have destroy all this country . and there have no old people die , nor Spaniard , but only this country people , from one year to 30 year of age : so for want of Indians we can not work in the mine . this sickness run all along the coast of Peru , and have pass into the strait of magalane , whence we have news that those soldier which be send from Spain
