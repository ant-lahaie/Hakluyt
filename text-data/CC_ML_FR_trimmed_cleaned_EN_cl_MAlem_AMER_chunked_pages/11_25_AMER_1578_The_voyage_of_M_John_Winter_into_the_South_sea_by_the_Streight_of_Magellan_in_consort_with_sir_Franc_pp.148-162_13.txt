And run along towards the northwest about 70 league , the wind turn direct against we , with great extremity of foul weather , as rain , hail , snow , and thick fog which continue so more than 3 week , that we can bear no sail , at which time we be drive 57 degr . to the south pole . the 15 of September the moon be there eclipse , and begin to be darken present after the set of the sun , about 6 of the clock at night , be then equinoctial vernal in that country . the say eclipse happen the 16 day in the morning before one of the clock in England , which be about six hour difference , agree to one quarter of the world , from the meridian of England towards the west . the last of September be a very foul night , and the sea sore grow , we lose the marigold , the general ship and the Elizabeth run to the eastward to get the shore , whereof we have sight , the 7 of October , fall into a very dangerous bay full of rock : and there we lose company of m .
