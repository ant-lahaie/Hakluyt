But the more to eternise the memory of his parents and to make his name renowned , he commanded that he should be served at the Table in vessels of gold and silver and others of earth : declaring thereby that the dignity wherein he was placed came not unto him by his parents , but by his own virtue only . If I shall speak of our time , I will lay before you only Rusten Bassha , which may be a sufficient example to all men : which though he were the son of a poor herdsman , did so apply his youth in all virtue , that being brought up in the service of the great Turke , he seemed to aspire to great and high matters , in such sort that growing in years he increased also in courage , so far forth , that in fine for his excellent virtues he married the daughter of the great Turke his Prince . Howe much then ought so many worthy examples to move you to plant here ? Considering also that hereby you shall registered for ever as the first that inhabited this strange country .
