There is a vein of earth along the sea coast for the space of forty or fifty miles , whereof by the judgement of some that have made trial here in England , is made good Alum , of that kind which is called Roch alum . The richness of such a commodity is so well known , that I need not to say any thing thereof . The same earth does also yield White coprasse , Nitrum , and Alumen plumeum , but nothing so plentifully as the common Alum , which be also of price , and profitable . Wapeih .
