Great was the care and diligence of our captain , master , and company to consume these vermin , but the more we laboured to kill them , the more they increased ; so that at the last we could not sleep for them , but they would eat our flesh , and bite like Mosquitos . In this woeful case , after we had passed the Equinoctial towards the North , our men began to fallen sick of such a monstrous disease , as I think the like was never herd of : for in their ankles it began to swell ; from thence in two days it would be in their breasts , so that they could not draw their breath , and then fell into their cods ; and their cods and yards did swell most grievously , and most dreadfully to behold , so that they could neither stand , lie , nor go .
