The second part of such commodities as Virginia is known to yield for victuali and sustenance of men life , usually fed upon by the natural inhabitants ; as also by us , during the time of our abided : and first of such as are sowed and husbanded . Agatowr , a kind of grain so called by the inhabitants : the same in the West Indies is called Mayz : English men call it Guiny - wheat or Turkey - wheat , according to the names of the countries ' from whence the like hath been brought . The grain is about the bigness of our ordinary English pease , and not much different in form and shape : but of diverse colours : some white , some read , some yellow , and some blue . All of them yield a very white and sweet flower : being used according to his kind , it makes a very good bred .
