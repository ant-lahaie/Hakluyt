But all the controversy consisteth in the Winter , for then the Sun leaveth those regions , and is no more seen for the space of other sixe months , in the which time all the Suns course is under their horizon for vii 273 s the space of half a year , and then those regions ( say some ) must needs be deformed with horrible darkness , and continual night , which may be the cause that beasts can not seek their food , and that also the cold should then be intolerable .
