Upon this Hand was found good store of the Over , which in the washing held gold to our thinking plainly to be seen : whereupon it was thought best rather to load here , where there was store and indifferent good , then to seek further for better , and spend time with jeoperdie . And therefore our general setting the Miners to work , and showing first a good president of a painful labourer and a good Captain in himself , gave good examples for others to follow him : whereupon every man both better and worse , with their best endeavours willingly laid to their helping hands . And the next day , being the thirtieth of July , the Michaell was sent over to Jackmans found , for the Ay de and the whole company to come thither .
