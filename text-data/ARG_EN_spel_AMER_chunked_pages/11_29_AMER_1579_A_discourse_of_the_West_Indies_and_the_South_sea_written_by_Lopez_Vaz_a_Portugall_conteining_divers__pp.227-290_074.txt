For the Spaniards in their discovery of these Islands not seeking nor being desirous of gold , brought home notwithstanding 40000 pezos with them , beside great store of cloves and ginger , and some cinnamon also which is not so good as in other places . The discoverer of these Islands nam them the Isles of Salomon , to the end that the Spaniards supposing them to be those Isles from whence Salomon fetched gold to adorn the temple at Jerusalem , might be the more desirous to go and inhabit the same .
