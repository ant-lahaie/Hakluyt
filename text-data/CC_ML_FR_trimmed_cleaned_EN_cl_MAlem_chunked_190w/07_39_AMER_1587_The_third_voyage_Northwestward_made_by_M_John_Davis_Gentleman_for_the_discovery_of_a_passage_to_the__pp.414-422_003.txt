June . the first 6 . day we have fair weather : after that for 5 . day we have fog and rain , the wind be south . the 12 . we have clear weather . the mariner in the sunshine and the master can not agree : the mariner will go on their voyage a fish , because the year begin to waste : the master will not depart till he have the company of the Elizabeth , whereupon the master tell our captain that he be afraid his man will shape some contrary course while he be asleep , and so he shall lose we . at length after much talk and many threaten , they be content to bring we to the land which we look for daily . the 14 . day we discover land at five of the clock land descry . in the morning , be very great and high mountain , the top of the hill be cover with snow . here the 415 wind be variable , sometime Northeast , Eastnortheast ,
