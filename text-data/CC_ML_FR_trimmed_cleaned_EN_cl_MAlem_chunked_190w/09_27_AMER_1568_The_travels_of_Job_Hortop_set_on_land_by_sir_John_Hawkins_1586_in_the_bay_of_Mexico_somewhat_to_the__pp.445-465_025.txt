James collier with his company that day pass the same river , and be also rob , and one of they slay by chance : we come that night unto the hill , where Anthony Goddard and his company rest , there we remain till morning , and then we march altogether from thence , enter between two grove , where the Indians rob we of all our clothes , and leave we naked , they hurt many , and kill eight of we . three day after we come to another river , there the indian show we the way to Panuco , and so leave we : we pass the river into the wilderness , where we make wreath of green grass , which we wound about our body , to keep we from the sun , and gnat of that country . we travel there seven day , and seven night , before we come to Panuco , feed on nothing but root , and guiavo , a fruit like
