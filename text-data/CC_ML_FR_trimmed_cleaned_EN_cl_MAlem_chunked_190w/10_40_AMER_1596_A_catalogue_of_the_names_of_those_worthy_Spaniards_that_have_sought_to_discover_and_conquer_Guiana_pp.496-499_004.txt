Theise province be of a excellent temperature , very healthful , and have a admirable influence in produce fair woman . the Spaniard afterwards to requite the manifold courtesy that they receive in that country , take and carry away , beside all the gold that they can get , all the Indians that they can lay hold on : they convey they in iron to Cubagua , and sell they for slave .
