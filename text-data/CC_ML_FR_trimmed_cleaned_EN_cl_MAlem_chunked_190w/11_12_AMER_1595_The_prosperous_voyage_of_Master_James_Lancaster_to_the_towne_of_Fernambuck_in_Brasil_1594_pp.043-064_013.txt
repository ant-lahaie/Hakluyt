Theise come to the fort or platform lie over against the entry of the harbour , and there attend our land : but before our admiral set forward with his boat , he give express order to all that have charge of govern the boat or galley , to run they with such violence against the shore , that they shall be all cast away without recovery , and not one man to stay in they , whereby our man may have no manner of retreat to trust unto , but only to God and their weapon . now be the time come of the flood , be about two of the clock in the afternoon , when our admiral set forward , and
