George , we give over our purpose to water there , and the next day frame our due course for England . October . the 2 . of October in the morning we see saint Michael hand on our star board quarter . the 23 . at 10 . of the clock afore noon , we see Ushant in Britaigne . on Saturday the 24 . we come in safety , God be thank , to a anchor at Plymmouth .
