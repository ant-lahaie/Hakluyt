Savages row after we , be by judgement 10 , league off from the shore : they bring we salmon peal , bird , and caplin , and we give they pin , needle , bracelet , nail , knife , bell , look glass , and other small trifle , and for a knife , a nail or a bracelet , which they call Ponigmah , they will sell their boat , coat , or any thing they have , although they be far from the shore . we have but few skin of they , about 20 . but they make sign to we that if we will go to the shore , we shall have more store of chichsanege : they
