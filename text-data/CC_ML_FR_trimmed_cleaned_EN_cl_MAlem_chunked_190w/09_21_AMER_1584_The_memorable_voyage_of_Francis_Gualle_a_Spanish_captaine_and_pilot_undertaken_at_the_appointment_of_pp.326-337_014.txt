Chap . 3 . the navigation or course of the aforesaid Francisco gualle out of the haven of Macao to new Spain , with the situation and stretching of the same , with other notable and memorable thing concern the same voyage . when we have prepare our self , and have take our leaf of our friend in Macao , we set sail upon the four and twenty of July , hold our course southeast , and southeast and by east , be in the wane of the moon : for when the moon increase , it be hard hold the course between the hand , because as then the water and stream run very strong to the northwest ; we travail through many narrow channel by night
