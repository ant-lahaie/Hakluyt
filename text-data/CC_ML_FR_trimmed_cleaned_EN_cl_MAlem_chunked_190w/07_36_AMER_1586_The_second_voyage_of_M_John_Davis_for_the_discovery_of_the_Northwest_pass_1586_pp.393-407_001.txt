the 29 . of June after many tempestuous storm we again discover land , in longitude from the meridian of London 58 . degr . 30 . min . and in latitude 64 . being east from we : into which course sith it please God by contrary wind to force we , i think it very necessary to bear in with it , and there to set up our pinnace , provide in the mermaid to be our scout for this discovery , and so much the rather because the year before i have be in the same place , and find it very convenient for such a purpose , well store with float wood , and possess by a people of tractable conversation : so that the 29 . of this month we arrive within the isle which lie before this land , lie north northwest , and south southeast , we know not how far . this land be very high and mountainous , have before it on the west side a mighty company of isle full of fair sound , and harborough . this land be very little
