Anthony the thirteenth of May , and proceed about the cape of Florida , we never touch any where ; but coast alongst Florida , and keep the shore still in sight , the 28 . of May early in the morning we descry on the shore a place build like a beacon , which be in deed a scaffold upon four long mast raise on end , for man to discover to the seaward , be in the latitude of thirty degree , or very near thereunto . our pinnace man , and come to the shore , we march up alongst the river side , to see what place the enemy hold there : for none among we have any knowledge thereof at all . here the general take occasion to march with the company himself
