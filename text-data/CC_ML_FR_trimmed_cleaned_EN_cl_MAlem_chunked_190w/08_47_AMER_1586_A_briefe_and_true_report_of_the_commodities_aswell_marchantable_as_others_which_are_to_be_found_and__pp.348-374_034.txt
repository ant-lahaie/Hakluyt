Kaishucpenauk , a white kind of root about the bigness of hen egg , and near of that form : their taste be not so good to our seem as of the other , and 364 1586 . therefore their place and manner of grow not so much care for by we : the inhabitant notwithstanding use to boil and eat many . Tsinaw , a kind of root much like unto that which in England be call the china root bring from the east Indies . and we know not any thing to the contrary but that it may be of the same kind .
