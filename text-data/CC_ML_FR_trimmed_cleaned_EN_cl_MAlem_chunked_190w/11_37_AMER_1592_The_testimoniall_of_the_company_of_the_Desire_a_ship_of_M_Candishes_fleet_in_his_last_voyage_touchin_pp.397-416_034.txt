So the allowance be two ounce and a half of meal for a man a day , and to have so twice a week , so that 5 ounce do serve for a week . three day a week we have oil , three spoonful for a man a day ; and 2 day in a week peason , a pint between 4 man a day , and every day 5 penguin for 4 man , and 6 quart of water for 4 man a day . this be our allowance ; wherewith ( we praise God ) we live , though weak , and very feeble .
