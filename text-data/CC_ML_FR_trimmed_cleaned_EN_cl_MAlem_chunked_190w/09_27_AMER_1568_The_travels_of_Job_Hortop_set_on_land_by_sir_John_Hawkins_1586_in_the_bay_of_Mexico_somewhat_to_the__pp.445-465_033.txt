Havana . with ginger grow in they , which be also send from China , to be send to the king of Spain . the ginger run in the ground like to liccoras , the blade grow out of it in length and proportion like unto the blade of wild garlic , which they cut every fifteen day , they use to water they twice a day , as we do our herb here in England , they put the blade in their pottage , and use they in their other meat , who excellent savour and taste be very delightful , and procure a good appetite . when
