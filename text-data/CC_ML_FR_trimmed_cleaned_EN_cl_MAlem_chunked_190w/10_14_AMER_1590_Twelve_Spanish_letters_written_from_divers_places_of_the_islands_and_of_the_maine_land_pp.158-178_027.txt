there have be no rain in this kingdom of long time . for in march the husbandman use to sow their corn : and in April winter do begin . and if in April there be no rain , the corn which be sow will consume away : and so for want of rain we have have two bid year of corn . and likewise here have be in these country of Potossi , and in the city de la paz , great sickness among the plague . Indians , Mullatos , and mestizo , call the small pock , and a certain plague , which have destroy all this country . and there have no old people die , nor Spaniard , but only this country people , from one year to 30 year of age : so for want of Indians we can not work in the mine . this sickness run all along the coast of Peru , and have pass into the strait of magalane , whence we have news that those soldier which be send from Spain thither to build those fort be most of they dead ,
