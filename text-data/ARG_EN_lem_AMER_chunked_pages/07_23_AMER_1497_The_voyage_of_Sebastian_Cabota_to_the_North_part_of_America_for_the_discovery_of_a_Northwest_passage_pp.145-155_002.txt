Sebastian Cabota Pilot mayor of Spain . when my father depart from Venice many year since to dwell in England , to follow the trade of merchandises , he take i with he to the city of London , while i be very young , yet have nevertheless some knowledge of letter of humanity , and of the sphere . 147 the second voyage of Cabot to the land of Brasil , and Rio the plat a . and when my father die in that time when news be bring that don Christopher Colonus Genuese have discover the coast of India , whereof be great talk in all the court of king Henry the 7 . who then reign , insomuch that all man with great admiration affirm it to be a thing more divine then humane , to sail by the west into the east where spice grow , by a way that be never know before , by this fame and report there increase in my heart a great flame of desire to attempt some notable thing .
