a letter of the say m . John Davis write to m . Sanderson of London concern his forewrite voyage . ood m . Sanderson , with God great mercy i have make my safe return in health , with all my company , and have sail threescore league further than my determination at my departure . i have be in 73 degree , find the sea all open , and forty league between land and land . the passage be most probable , the execution easy , as at my come you shall full know . yesterday the 15 of September i land all weary ; therefore i pray you pardon my shortness . Sandridge this 16 of September anno 1587 . you equal as i own , which by trial you shall best know , John Davis . .
