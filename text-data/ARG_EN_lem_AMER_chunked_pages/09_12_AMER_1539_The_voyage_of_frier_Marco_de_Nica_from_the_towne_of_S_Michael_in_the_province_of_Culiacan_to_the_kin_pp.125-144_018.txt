Chap . 5 . the situation and greatness of the city of Cevola , and how friar Marcus take possession thereof and of other province , call the same . the new kingdom of saint Francis , and how after his departure from thence be preserve by God in so dangerous a voyage , he arrive at Compostella in Nueva Galicia . have consider the former report of the Indians , and the evil mean which i have to prosecute my voyage as i desire , i think it not good wilful to loose my life as Stephan do : and so i tell they , that God will punish those of Cevola , and that the viceroy when he shall understand what have happen , will send many Christians to chastise they : but they will not believe i , for they say that no man be able to withstand the power of Cevola . and herewithal i leave they , and go aside two or three stone cast , and when i return i find a indian of i which i have bring from Mexico call Marcus , who weep and say unto i : father , these man have consult to kill we , for they say , that through your and Stephans mean their father be slay , and that neither man nor woman of they shall remain unslain . 141 A .
