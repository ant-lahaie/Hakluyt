And pass between Chagre and Veragua , we take a frigate , and some Veragua . quantity of gold we find therein . in this frigate be 23 Spaniard who we set on shore , and two Fleming , they we bring into England with we : we have therein also four cast piece of ordinance , 3 harquebuss on crock , 16 spanish caliver , and a book of navigation : and in this frigate some of our company come homeward into England . thus pass forward in our course , we come to Veragua , where captain Barker , and Philip Roche his master , fight upon the foresay quarrel , in which combat the captain be hurt a little in the cheek . here we sink our admiral the ragged staff , because of her great leakage , and embark part of our company in the spanish frigate , which immediate before we have surprise . from hence ( by the direction of certain Indians ) we sail into the gulf of the Honduras : the bay of there we take a bark wherein be rial of plate , to honduras - the value of 100 pound , Maiz or indian wheat , hen , beef and other small thing . in this bark also be certain Spaniard , whereof one be the Scrivano or a conspiracy against the captain . m .
