Truly God wrought marvellous for they , for they themselves have no victual but water , and be sore oppress with hunger , be not of opinion to bestow any further time in seek the ship then that present noon time ; so that if they have not at that instant espy they , they have go to the shore to have make provision for victual , and with such thing as they can have get , either to have go for that part of Florida where the french man be plant ( which will have be very hard for they to have do , because they want victual to bring they thither , be a hundred and twenty league off7 ) or else to have remain among the floridians ; at who hand they be put in comfort by a french man , who be with they , that have remain in Florida at the first find thereof , a whole year together , to receive victual sufficient , and gentle entertainment , if need be , for a year or two , until which time God may have provide for they . but how contrary this will have fall out to their expectation
