A voyage of one Pudsey to Baya in Brasil anno 1542.{n}Lso the worshipfull M. Edward Cotton of Southampton Esquire gave mee more particularly to understand, how that one Pudsey of Southampton, a man of good skill and resolution in marine causes, made a voyage in like maner 62. yeeres agoe to Baya de{n}todos los Santos the principali towne of all Brasil, and the seate of the Portugal vice-roy and of the bishop, and that he built a fort not farre distant from that place, in the foresaid yeere 1542.{n}A fort built in Brasil by the English.{n}.
