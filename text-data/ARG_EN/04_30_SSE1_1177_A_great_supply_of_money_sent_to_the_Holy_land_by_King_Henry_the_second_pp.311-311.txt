A great supply of money to the Holy land by Henry the 2.{n}A.D.{n}1177.{n}[II. i. 18.]{n}He same yeere King Henry the second being at Waltham, assigned an aide to the maintenance of the Christian souldiers in the Holy lande, That is{n}to wit, two and fortie thousand markes of silver, and five{n}hundred markes of golde. Matth. Paris, and Helens.{n}.
