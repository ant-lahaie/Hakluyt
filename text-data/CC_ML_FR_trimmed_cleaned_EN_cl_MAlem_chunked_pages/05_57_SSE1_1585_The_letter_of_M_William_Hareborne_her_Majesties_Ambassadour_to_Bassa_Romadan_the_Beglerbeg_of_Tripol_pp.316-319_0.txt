a letter of master William hareborne , the english ambassador , Ligier in Constantinople , to the bassa Romadan , the Beglerbeg of Tripoli in Barbary , for the restore of a english ship call the Jesus , with the good , and man , detain as slave , anno 1585 - 317 [ ii i . 194 . ] right honourable lord , it have be signify unto we by diverse letter , what have fall out , concern a certain ship of we , call the Jesus , into which , for the help of Richard skeg , one of our merchant in the same , now decease , there be admit a certain Frenchman call roman sonning , which for his ill behaviour , accord to his desert , seek to carry away with he another Frenchman , which be indebt to certain of your people , without pay his cteditour , be hang by sentence of justice , together with Andrew Dier , the master of the say ship , who simple and without fraud , give credit to the say Frenchman , without any knowledge of his evil fact , do not return when he be command , by your honourable lordship . the death of the say lewd Frenchman we approve as a thing well do , but contrariwise , whereas your lordship have confiscate the say ship with the good therein , and have make slave of the mariner , as a thing altogether contrary to the privilege of the grand signior , grant four year since , and confirm by we on the behalf of the most excellent the queen majesty of England our mistress , and altogether contrary to the league of the say grand signior , who be full inform of the aforesaid cause , have grant unto we his royal commandment of restitution , which we send unto your honourable lordship , by the present bearer Edward Barton our secretary , and Mahomet beg , one of the justice of his stately court , with other letter of the most excellent admiral , and most valiant captain of the sea , require your honourable lordship , as well on the behalf of the grand signior , as of the queen most excellent majesty my mistress , that the man , oil , ship , furniture , money , and all other good whatsoever , by your lordship , and your order
