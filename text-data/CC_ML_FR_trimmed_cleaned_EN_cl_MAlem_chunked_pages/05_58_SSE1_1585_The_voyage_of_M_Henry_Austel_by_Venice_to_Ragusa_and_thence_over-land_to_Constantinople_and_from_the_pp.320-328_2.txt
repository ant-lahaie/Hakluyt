Venice . the 18 . in the morning we come to Venice , and there we stay 15 . day . in which time the duke of Venice call Nicholas de Ponte die , and we see his burial . the senator be continual shut up together , as the manner be , to choose a new duke , which be not yet choose when we depart from thence . the 2 . of August at night we do embark our self upon the frigate of Cattaro , a haven near Ragusa . the 3 . we come to a town in Istria call Citta nova . the 4 . we come to Parenzo , and so that night to Forcera of the bishop . the 5 . we pass by Rovigno : and a little beyond we meet with 3 . galies of the venetian : we pass in the sight of Pola ; and the same day pass the gulf that part Istria from Dalmatia . the 6 . of August we come to Zara in Dalmatia , a strong town of the venetian : and so that night to Sebenico , which stand in a marvelous goodly haven , with a strong castle at the entry thereof . the 7 . we come to Lezina , and go not on shore , but travel all night . the 8 . we pass by a very well seat town call Curzola , which stand in a island of that name . 322 the 9 in the morning betimes we land at Ragusa , Ragusa . and there stay three day , where we find many friendly gentleman . the 11 . be provide of a janizary we depart from Ragusa in the company of half a dozen merchant of that town : and
