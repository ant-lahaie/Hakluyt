Thomas on the one side , and the arm of Portugal on the other side . there be also a kind of money call cruzado stamp with the arm of Portugal , and be worth 6 tangas good money , the larine be every 9 of they worth 2 cruzado , which be 12 tangas good money , and these larines be of those which be stamp in Balsara and Ormuz . the royal of 8 they call pardaos de Reales , and be worth 7 tangas of good money . the custom of Malacca be 10 in the 100 as well inward as outward , and those which pay the custom inward , if in case they send the same good for any other place within term of a year and a day , pay no custom for the same .
