Chap . 4 . the mongal or Tartar , in outward shape , be unlike to all other people . for they be broad between the eye , and the ball of their cheek , than man of other nation be . they have flat and small nose , little eye , and eye lid stand straight upright , they be shave on the crown like priest . they wear their hair somewhat long about their ear , then upon their forehead : but behind they let it grow long like woman hair , whereof they bray two lock bind each of they behind either ear . they have short foot also . the garment , as well of their man , as of their woman be all of one fashion . they
