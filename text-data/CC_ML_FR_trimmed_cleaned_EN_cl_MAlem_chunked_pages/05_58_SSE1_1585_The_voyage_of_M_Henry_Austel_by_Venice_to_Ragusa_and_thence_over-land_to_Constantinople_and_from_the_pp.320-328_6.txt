Breslaw . the second to dinner at lanczut , at night to Retsbou . the third to Sendxizow , at night to Tarnow , and that night we meet with the palatine Laski . the four to Vonuez , and that night to Brytska . the five to Kuhena . the 6 . to Cracovia the principali city of all Poland : at which time the king be go to Lituania : for he do make his residence one year in Poland , and the other in Lituania . Cracovia stand on the river of Vistula . the 9 we depart from Cracovia , and that night we come to a village hard by a town call Ilkusch , where the lead mine be . the 10 . we pass by a town call slawkow : where there be also lead mine , and bait that day at Bendzin , which be the last town of Poland towards Silesia ; and there be a toll . note that all the country of Poland , Russia alba , podolia , Bogdania , and diverse other country adjoin unto they , do consume no other salt but such as be dig in Sorstyn mountain near to Cracovia which be as hard as any stone ; it be very good , and go further than any other salt . that night we lie at bitom , which be the first town of Silesia . the 12 . we pass by a great town call Strelitz , and that night we lie at Oppelen upon the river of Odera . the 13 . we pass by schurgasse , and that night we lie without the town of Brigk : for we can not be suffer to come in by reason of the plague which be in those part in diverse town . the 14 . we pass by olaw , and that night we come to the city of Breslaw , which be a fair town , great , well build and well seat upon the river of Odera . the 16 . we bait at Neumargt . the 17 . we pass by Lignizt and by Hayn , and that night to Buntzel . the 18 . we pass by Naum burg through Gorlitz 326 upon the river of Neiss , and that night lie without Reichenbach . the
