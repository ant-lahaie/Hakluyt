This Vologhda be in 59 degree , eleven minute , and be from colmogro , 1000 verstes . all the way i never come in house , but lodge in the wilderness , by the river side , and carry provision for the way . and he that will travel those way , must good counsel ! carry with he a hatchet , a tinder box , and a kettle , for it ™ ™ 116 ™ ' to make fire and seethe meat , when he have it : for there be small succour in those part , unless it be in town . 419 the first day of December , i depart from Vologhda in post in a sled , as the manner be in winter . and the way to Moscua be as follow . from Vologhda to Commelski , 27 verstes , so to Olmor 25 verstes , so to teloytske 20 verstes , so to ure 30 verstes
