D . 1553 signor himself be lodge within the town , in a goodly castle , situate upon a high mountain : at the foot whereof run a goodly river , which be a branch of that famous river Euphrates . the rest of his army pass over the mountain of Armenia call now the mountain of carnarie , which be four day journey from Aleppo , appoint there to tarry the come of the grand signior , with the rest of his army , intend to march into Persia , to give battle to the great sophy . So the whole army of the grand signior , contain as well those that go by the mountain , as also those that come to Aleppo in company with he , with horseman and footman , and the conductor of the camel and victual , be the number of 300000 . man . the camel which carry munition and victual for the say army , be in number 200000 .
