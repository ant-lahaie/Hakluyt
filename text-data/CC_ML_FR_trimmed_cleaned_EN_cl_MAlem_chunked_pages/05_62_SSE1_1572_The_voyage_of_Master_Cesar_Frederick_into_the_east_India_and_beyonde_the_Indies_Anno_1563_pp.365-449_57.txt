And return to my purpose , i say , be among these rock , and far from the land which be over against tanasary , with great scarcity of victual , and that by the say of the pilot and two Portugal , hold then firm that we be in front of the aforesaid harbour , we determine to go thither with our boat and fetch victual , and that the ship shall stay for we in a place assign . we be twenty and eight person in the boat that go for victual , and on a day about twelve of the clock we go from the ship , assure our self to be in the harbour before night in the aforesaid port , we row all that day , and a great part of the next night , and all the next day without find harbour , or any sign of good land , and this come to pass through the evil counsel of the two Portugal that be with we . for we have overshoot the harbour and leave it
