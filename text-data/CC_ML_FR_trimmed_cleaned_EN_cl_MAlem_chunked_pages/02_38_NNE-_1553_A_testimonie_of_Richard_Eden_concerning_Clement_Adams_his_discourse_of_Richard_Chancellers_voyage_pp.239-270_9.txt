And be come thither , and have stay there the space of 7 . day , and look in vain for their come , he determine at length to proceed alone in the purpose voyage . and as he be prepare himself to depart , it happen that he fall in company and speech with certain scottishman : who have understanding of his intention , and wish well to his action , begin earnest to dissuade he from the further prosecution of the discovery , by amplify the danger which he be to fall into , and omit no reason that may serve to that purpose . but he hold nothing so ignominious and reproachful , as inconstancy and levity of mind , and persuade himself that a man of valour can not commit a more dishonourable part then for fear of danger to avoid and shun great attempt , be nothing at all change or discourage with the speech and word of the Scot , remain steadfast and immutable in his first resolution : determine either to bring that to pass which be intend , or else to die the death . 247 they arrive in the bay of saint Nicholas . and as for they which be with master chancellor in his ship , although they have great cause of discomfort by the loss of their company ( who the foresay tempest have separate from they , ) and be not a little trouble with cogitation and perturbation of mind , in respect of their doubtful course :
