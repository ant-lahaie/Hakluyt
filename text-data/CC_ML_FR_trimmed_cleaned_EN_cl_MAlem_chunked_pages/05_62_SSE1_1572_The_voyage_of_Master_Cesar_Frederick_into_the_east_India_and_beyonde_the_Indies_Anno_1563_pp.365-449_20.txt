Theise brethren be three captain belong to the father of the king they keep in prison , which when he die , leave his son very young , and then they take the government to themselves . the chief of these three be call ramaragio , and sit in the royal throne , and be call the king : the second be call temiragio , and he take the government on he : the three be call bengatre , and he be captain general of the army .
