Olavus therefore be at liberty come unto man , and immediate with a great company of noble take his journey unto saint James : and his brother Reginald cause the say Olavus to take unto wife , the daughter of a certain noble man of Kentyre , cousine germane unto his own wife , and by name be call Lavon , and he grant unto he the possession of Lewis . after a few day Reginald the bishop of the island have gather a synod , separate Olavus and godr his son , and Lavon his wife , namely because she be cousin germane unto his former wife . afterwards Olavus marry Scristina daughter unto Ferkarus earl of Rosse . hereupon the wife of Reginald queen of the island be incense , send letter unto the island of sky in king Reginald his name to her son godred will he to take Olavus . which commandment godred put in practice , and enter the isle of Lewis for you same purpose , Olavus flee in a little skiff unto his father in law the earl of Rosse , and in the mean time godred waste the isle of Lewis . at the very same time Pol the son of book 35 the isle of man advanced to a kingdom . viscount of sky , be a man of power in all the island , because he will not consent unto godred , flee , and dwell together with Olavus in the dominion of the earl of Rosse , and make a league with Olavus , they go both in a ship unto sky . to be short , send certain spy , they be inform that godred remain secure with a small company in a certain isle call you isle of saint Columba . And unite unto themselves their friend and acquaintance , and other that will go voluntary with they , in the dead of the night , have launch 5 . ship from the next seashore , which be distant about the space of 2 . furlong from the foresay
