a copy of the first privilege grant by the emperor of Russia to the english merchant in the year 1555 . JOhn Vasilivich , by the grace of God emperor of Russia , great duke of Novogrode , Moscovia , etc to all people that shall see , read , hear or understand these present , greeting . forasmuch as God have plant all realm and dominion in the whole world with sundry commodity , so as the one have need of the amity and commodity of the other , and by mean thereof traffic be use from one to another , and amity thereby increase : and for that as among man nothing be more to be desire then amity , without the which no creature be of a natural good disposition can live in quietness , so that it be as troublesome to be utter want , as it be perceive to be grievous to the body to lack air , fire , or any other necessary most requisite for the conservation and maintenance thereof in health : consider also how needful merchandise be , which furnish man of all that which be convenient for their live and nurture , for their clothing , trim , the satisfy of their delight , and all other thing convenient and profitable for they , and that merchandise bring the same commodity from diverse quarter in so great abundance , as by mean thereof nothing be lack in any part , and that all thing be in every place ( where intercourse of merchandize be receive and embrace ) general in such sort , as amity thereby be enter into , and plant to continue , and the injoyer thereof be as man live in a golden world : upon these respect and other weighty and good consideration , we hereunto move , and chief upon the contemplation of the gracious letter , direct from the right high , right excellent , and right mighty queen mary , by the grace of God queen of England , France , etc in the favour of her subject , merchant , the governor , consul , assistant , and commonalty of merchant adventurer for discovery of land , etc know you therefore , that we of our grace speciali , mere motion , and certain knowledge , have give and grant , and by these present for we ,
