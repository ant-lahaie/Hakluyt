the honourable and prosperous voyage of Richard earl of Cornwall , brother to king Henry the three , accompany with William longe - spee earl of Sarisburie , and many other noble man into Syria . 1240 . n the 24 year of king Henry the three , Richard earl of Cornwall the king brother , with a navy of ship sail into Syria , where in the war against the Saracen he great advance the part of the Christians . there go over with he the earl of Sarisburie , William longspee , and William Basset , John Beauchampe , Geffrey de Lucy , John Nevel , Geffrey Beauchampe , Peter de Brense , and William Furnivall . Simon Montfort earl of Leicester go over also the same time : but whereas the earl of Cornwall take the sea at Marseils , the earl of Leicester pass through Italy , and take ship at brindize in Apulia : and with he go these person of name , Thomas the fur - nival with his brother Gerard de Furnivall , Hugh wake , almerike de saint Aumond , wiscard Ledet , Punchard de Dewin , and William de Dewin that be brethren , Gerard Pesmes , Fouke de Baugie , and Peter de Chauntenay . short after also John earl of Albemarle , William fortis , and Peter the mallow a Poictouin , man for their valiancy great renown , go thither , lead with they a great number of christian soldier .
