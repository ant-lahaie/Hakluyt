Furthermore , if any man bring a letter unto he , he may not open it , but in the presence of the signiory , and they be to see it first , which be read , perhaps they will deliver it to he , perhaps not . of the signiory there be about three hundred , and about fourty of the privy counsel of Venice , who usual be array in gown of crimson satin , or crimson damask , when they sit in counsel . in the city of Venice , no man may wear a weapon , except he be a soldier for the signiory , or
