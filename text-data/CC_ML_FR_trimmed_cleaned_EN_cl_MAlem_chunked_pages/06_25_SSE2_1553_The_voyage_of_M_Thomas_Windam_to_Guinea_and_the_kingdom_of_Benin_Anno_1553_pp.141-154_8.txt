Pinteado give the foresay counsel to go no further for the safeguard of the man and their life , which they shall put in danger if they come too late , for the rossia which be their winter , not for cold , but for smother heat , with close and cloudy air and storm weather , of such putrify quality , that it rot the coat of their back : or else for come to soon for the scorch heat of the sun , which cause they to linger in the way . but of force and not of will bring he the ship before the river of Benin , where ride at a anchor , they send their pinnace up into the river 50 or 60 league , from whence certain of the merchant with captain Pinteado , Francisco a Portugal , Nicholas Lambart gentleman , and other merchant be conduct to the court where the king remain , ten league from the 148 river side , whither when they come , they be bring with a great company to the presence of the king , who the king of be a black moor ( although not so black as the rest ) Bentn be sit
