Even from their childhood they wear dagger and sword , the which they use to lay under their pillow when they go to bed : in show courteous and affable , in deed haughty and proud . they delight most in warlike affair , and their great study be arm . Mens apparel diverse colour be wear down half the leg and to the elbow : woman attire make handsome like unto a veil , be somewhat long : all manner of dice and theft they do eschew . the merchant , although he be wealthy , be not account of .
