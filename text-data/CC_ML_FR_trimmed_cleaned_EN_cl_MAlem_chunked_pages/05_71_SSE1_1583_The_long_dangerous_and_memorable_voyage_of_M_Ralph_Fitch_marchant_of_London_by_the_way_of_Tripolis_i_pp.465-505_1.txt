Felugia where you unlade your good which come from birra be a little village : from whence you go to Babylon in a day . Babylon . Babylon be a town not very great but very populous , and of great traffic of stranger , for that it be the way to Persia , Turkia and Arabia : and from thence do go Carovans for these and other place . here be great store of victual , which come from Armenia down the river of Tigris . they be bring upon raft make of goat skin blow full of wind and board lay upon they : and thereupon they lade their good which be bring down to Babylon , which be discharge they open their skin , and carry they back by camel , to serve another time . Babylon in time past do belong to the kingdom of Persia , but now be subject to the Turk
