And see that it be a 28 most beautiful island , it please he exceed well , and therefore he make choice to inhabit therein his own self , and build fort there which be at this day call by his own name . he have the people of Galway in such awe , that he constrain they to cut down their own timber , and to bring it unto his shore for the build of his fort . he sail on further unto the isle of Anglesey near unto wale , and find two earl therein ( either of they be call by the name of Hugo ) he slay the one , and the other he put to flight , and so subdue the island . but the Welshman present many gift unto he , and so bid they farewell he return unto man unto Murecard king of Irland he send his shoe , command he that he shall carry they on his shoulder , upon the birthday of our lord through the midst of his palace , in the sight of his ambassador , that thereby it may appear unto they , that he be subject unto king magnus . which when the Irishman hear , they take it grievous and disdain much thereat . but the king be better advise , i have rather ( say he ) not only bear his shoe , but eat his shoe , than that king Magnus shall destroy any one province in Irland . wherefore he fulfil his commandment , and honourable entertain his ambassador . many gift also he send unto king magnus by they , and conclude a league . but the messenger return unto their lord , tell he of the situation of Irland , of the beauty thereof , of the fruitfulness of the soil , and of the holesomnesse of the air . Magnus hear these thing be full resolve to conquer all Irland unto himself . and for the same purpose he command that a fleet shall be make ready . but he take his voyage
