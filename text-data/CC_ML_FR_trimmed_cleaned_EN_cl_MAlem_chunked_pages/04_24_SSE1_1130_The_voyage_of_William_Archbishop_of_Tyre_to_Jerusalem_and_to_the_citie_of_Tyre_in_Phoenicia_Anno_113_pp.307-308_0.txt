the life and travail of one William of Tyre , a Englishman . Centur . 13 . William the prior of the canon regular in the church of Jerusalem , call the lord sepulchre , be a Englishman bear , and of a virtuous and good behaviour . after that the city of tyre be restore again to the christian faith , Guimunde the patriarch of Jerusalem make he the first archbishop of tyre , in the year 1128 . which tyre be a very ancient city , the metropolis of all Phoenicia , and have be account the chief province of Syria , both for fruitful commodity and multitude of inhabitant . This William have in his life write many book and epistle , die at last in the year 1130 . have be archbishop the space of two year , and be bury in the church of tyre .
