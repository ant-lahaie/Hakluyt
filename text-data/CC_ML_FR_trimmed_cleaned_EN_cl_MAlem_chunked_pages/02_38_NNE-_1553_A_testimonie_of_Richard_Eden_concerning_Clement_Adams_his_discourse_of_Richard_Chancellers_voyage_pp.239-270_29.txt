so little account do they make of liberty . 264 if any man be take upon commit of theft , he be imprison , and often beat , but not hang for the first offence , as the manner be with we : and this they call the law of mercy . he that offend the second time have his nose cut off , and be burn in the forehead with a hot iron . the three time , he be hang . there be many cutpurse among they , and if the rigour of the prince do not cut they off , they can not be avoid . of their religion . they maintain the opinion of the greek church : they suffer no grave image of saint in their church , but their picture paint in table they have in great abundance , which they do adore and offer unto , and burn wax candle before they , and cast holy water upon they , without other honour . they say that our image which be set up in church , and carve , have no divinity in they . in their private house they have image for their household saint , and for the most part , they be put in the dark place of the house : he that come into his neighbour house do first salute his saint , although he see they not . if any foorme or stool stand in his way , he oftentimes beat his brow upon the same , and often duck down with his head , and body , worship the chief image . the habit , and attire of the priest , and of the lay man , do nothing at all differ : as for marriage , it be forbid to no man : only this be receive and hold among they for a rule , and custom , that if a priest wife do die , he may not marry again , nor take a second wife : and therefore they of secular priest , as they call they , be make monk , to who then chastity for ever
