And be in his coven at Udene in the province of Padua , the ten day after the foresay vision , have receive the communion , and prepare himself unto God , yea , be strong and sound of body , he happy rest in the lord : who sacred departure be signify unto the [ ii i . 67 . ] 443 pope aforesaid , under the hand of the public notary in these word follow . in the year of our lord 1331 , the 14 . day of Januarie , Beatus Odoricus a friar minorite decease in Christ , at who prayer God show many and sundry miracle , which i guetelus public notary of utina , son of m . Damianus de porto Gruaro , at the commandment and direction of the honourable Conradus of the borough of Gastaldion , and one of the council of utina , have write as faithful as i can , and have deliver a copy thereof unto the friar minorite : howbeit not of all , because they be innumerable , and too difficult for i to write .
