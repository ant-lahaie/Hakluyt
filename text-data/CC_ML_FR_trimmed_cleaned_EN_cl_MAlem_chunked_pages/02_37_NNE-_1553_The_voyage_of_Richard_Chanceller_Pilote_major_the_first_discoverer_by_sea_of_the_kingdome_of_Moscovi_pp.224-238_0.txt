Russia be very plentiful both of land and people , and also wealthy for such commodity as they have . they be very great fisher for salmon and small cod : they have much oil which we call treine oil , the most whereof be make by a river call two Duina . they make it in other place , but not so much as there . they have also a great trade in seethe of salt water . to the north part of that country be the place where they have their fur , as sable , martern , grief Bevers , fox white , black , and red , minx , ermine , Miniver , and heart . there be also a fish tooth , which fish be call a morsse . the taker thereof dwell in a place call 224 1553 - postesora , which bring they upon heart to Lampas to sell , and from Lampas carry they to a place call Colmogro , where the high market be hold on saint Nicholas day . to the west of Colmogro there be a place call Gratanove , in our language novogorode , where much fine flax and hemp grow , and also much
