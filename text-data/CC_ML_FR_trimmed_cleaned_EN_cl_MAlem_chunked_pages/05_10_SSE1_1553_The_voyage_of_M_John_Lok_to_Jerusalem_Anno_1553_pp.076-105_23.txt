Thence we return to new Famagusta again to dinner , and towards evening we go about the town , and in the great church we see the tomb of king Jaques , which be the last king of Cyprus , and [ ii i . 109 . ] Cornari , a family of Venice marl to king Jaques , no victual must be sell out of the city of Famagusta . be bury in the year of Christ one thousand four hundred seventie and three , and have to wife one of the daughter of Venice , of the house of Cornari , the which family at this day have great revenue in this island , and by mean of that marriage the venetian challenge the kingdom of Cyprus . the first of October in the morning , we go to see the relief of the watch . that do , we go to one of the greek church to see a pot or jar of stone , which be say to be one of the seven jar of water , the which the lord God at the marriage convert into wine . it be a pot of earth very fair , white enamel , and fair wrought upon with draw work , and have on either side of it , instead off handle , ear make in form as the painter make angel wing , it be about a ell high , and small at the bottom , with a long neck and correspondent in circuit to the bottom , the belly very great and round , it hold full twelve gallon , and have a taphole to draw wine out thereat , the jar be very ancient , but whether it be
