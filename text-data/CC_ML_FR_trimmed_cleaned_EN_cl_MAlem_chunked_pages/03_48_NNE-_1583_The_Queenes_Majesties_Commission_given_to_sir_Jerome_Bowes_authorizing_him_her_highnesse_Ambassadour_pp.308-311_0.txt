Elizabeth by the grace of God , queen of England , France and Ireland , defender of the faith , etc to all and singular , to who these present shall come to be see and read , greeting . whereas the most excellent prince John Basiliwich king , and great duke of all Russia , Volodomer , Moscovie , and Novogrod , king of Cazan and astracan , lord of Plesco , and great duke of Smolensco , of tuer , Ugor , and Permia , Valca , Bolhar and other , lord great duke of Novogrod in the low country , of Cherniga , rezan , Polotsco , Rostove , yeraslave , Bealozera , Liefland , Oudor , Obdor and condensa , and governor of all the land of Siberia , and of the north part and other , our most dear brother and friend do of late send unto we one Feodor Andrewich Spisemsky , a noble man of he , to deal with we in certain speciali business , respect very near the honour of either of we , and be such as without the speed of some ambassador of we to the foresay most excellent prince , can be sufficient determine and conclude : for this cause we have great confidence in the fidelity , industry , provident circumspection and convenient experience of our well - beloved servant Jerome Bowes knight , a gentleman of quality of our household , do by these present make and constitute the foresay Jerome Bowes knight our true and undoubted ambassador , orator and special commissioner , give and grant to the same Jerome bow knight , our orator , by the virtue of these present authority and command - 310 commission to sir Jerome Bowes a . d . 1583 - mean , as well general as special , so that the special shall not prejudice the generail , nor on the other side the general the special , to entreat , confer , conclude , and appoint in our name , and for we with the foresay most excellent prince and his counseller and deputy whatsoever , concern the foresay business , and each of they , accord as it shall seem good , and for our honour to our foresay orator , as also off and upon such thing entreat , conclude and appoint , as in all and singular other thing , any manner of way concern the premise , to deliver in our name and for we , sufficient and effectual letter and instrument , and to require letter and instrument of the like validity and effect of the other part , and to procure they lawful to be make and seal , and then to receive they , and general to do , execute , and dispatch all and singular other thing concern the premise , in , and after the same manner , as we our self will and may do if we be present , although they be such thing as may seem of themselves to require a moor speciali commandement : promise in good faith and in the word of a prince , that we will hold and observe all and singular the thing which by our ambassador aforesaid shall be appoint , promise , agree , accord and conclude in this behalf , as lawful , grateful , and firm , and thereupon as need shall require , will give our letter patent , confirmatory and approbatory , in form effectual and authentical . in witness whereof , we have cause our great seal of our kingdom of England to be put to these present , and sign they with our own hand . give at our palace of Greenwich the four day of June , in the year of our lord 1583 , and of our reign the five and twenty .
