Theise all go clothe in green , or at the least have their turban green , to be know from the other . neither be it permit that any of those Christians which 351 dwell or traffic in their country go clothe in green , neither may they have any thing of green about they : for they say it be not lawful for misbeleevers - to wear that colour , wherein that great friend and prophet of God Mahomet be wont to be apparel . of the city of Mecca . the ci tie of Mecca in the arabian tongue be call Macca , that be to say , a habitation . this city be environ about with exceed high and barren mountains , and in
