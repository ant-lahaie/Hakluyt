Pliny and Soline write , that they use none adultery . if they happen to meet with a man in wilderness be out of the way , gentle they will go before he , and bring he into the plain way . join in battle , they have no small respect unto they that be wound : for they bring they that be hurt or weary into the middle of the army to be defend : they be make tame by drink the juise of barley .
