Furthermore , it be far more requisite that we shall carry our own commodity , than to suffer a stranger to carry they thither , for that we can afford they better cheap than a stranger can . 1 write not this by hearsay of other man , but of my own experience , for i have trade in the country above this 30 year , and have be marry in the town of Chio full 24 year , so that you may assure yourself that i will write nothing but truth . now i will declare unto you the ware and commodity that be in the country near about Chio . there be great store of very good gall , the best sort whereof be sell in sundrycom - England five shilling dear than any other country â€˜ gall . there be also cotton wool , tan hide , hide in the hair , wax , chamlet , mocayare , grogerams , silk of diverse country , cordovan skin tan white , to be make black , of they great quantity , and also course wool to make bed . the natural commodity grow in the hand it self be silk raw , and mastic . of these commodity there be lade yearly ten or twelve great ship of Genoa , beside five or six that do belong to the town of Chio , which ship be fraught for Genoa , Messina , and Ancona . and now that the mauneses and the chief merchant of Genoa be banish , the trade be clean lose , by reason whereof merchandise must now of necessity be better cheap than they have be in time past . but yet when all those ship do trade to the country , and also our ship , we never have less
