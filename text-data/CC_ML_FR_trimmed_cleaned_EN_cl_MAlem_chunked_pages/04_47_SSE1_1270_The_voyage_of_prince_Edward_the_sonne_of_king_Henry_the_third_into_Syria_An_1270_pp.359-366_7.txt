And accord to the promise he make the prince , it come to pass , to the no little comfort and admiration of all his subject . when the great sultan hear hereof , and that the prince be yet alive , he can scarce believe the same , and send unto he three of his noble and prince , excuse himself by they , call his god to witness that the same be do neither by he nor his 365 consent . which prince and messenger stand aloof off from the king son , worship he , fall flat upon the ground : you ( say the prince ) do reverence i , but yet you love i not . but they understand he not , because he speak in English unto they , speak by a interpreter : nevertheless he honourable entertain they , and send they away in peace . thus when prince Edward have be eighteen month in Aera , he take ship about the assumption of our lady , as we call it , return homeward , and after seven week he arrive in Sicilia at trape , and from thence travail through the mid of Apulia , till he come to roam , where he be of the pope honorable entertain . from thence he come into France , who fame and noble prowess be there much bruit among the common people , and envy of the nobility , especial of the earl of Chalons , who think to have entrap he and his company , as may appear in the story : but prince Edward continue forth his journey to Paris , and be there of the french king honourable entertain : and after certain day he go thence into Gascon , where he tarry till that he hear of the death of the king his father , at which time he come home , and be crown king of England , in the year of our lord 1274 .
