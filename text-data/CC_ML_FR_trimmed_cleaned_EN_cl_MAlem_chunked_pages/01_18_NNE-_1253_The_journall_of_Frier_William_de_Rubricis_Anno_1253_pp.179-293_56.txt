Theise hungarian be a great comfort unto we , bring we Cosmos to drink , yea , and sometime flesh for to eat also : who , when they request to have some book of we , and i have not any to give they ( for indeed we have none but only a bible , and a breviary ) it grieve i exceed . and i say unto they : bring i some ink and paper , and i will write for you so long as we shall remain here : and they do so . and i copy out for they horas beatae virginis , and officium defunctorum .
