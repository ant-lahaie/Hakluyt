Babylon and Basora . from Babylon i depart for Basora , ship my self in one of the bark that use to go in the river Tigris from Babylon to Basora , and from Basora to Babylon : which bark be make after the manner of fust or galliot with a speron and a cover poop : they have no pump in they because of the great abundance of pitch which they have to pitch they with all : which pitch they have in abundance two day journey from Babylon .
