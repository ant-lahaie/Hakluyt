Moreover there be every year certain magistrate appoint in each province , to go unto the king : and every three year all the governor of city and of town do visit he at once , what time trial be make of they that aspire unto the three degree : upon which occasion there be at the same time a incredible number of people at the king court . by reason of this excellent order and harmony of magistrate place one under another , it can scarce be imagine , what sweet peace and tranquillity flourish throughout the whole realm , especial since , after speedy inquisition , person that be guilty be put ( as the manner be there ) to the punishment of the bastonado : neither yet be suit or action any long time delay . also it be not to be omit , that for the obtain of any dignity or magistracy , the way be open , without all respect of gentry or blood , unto all man ,
