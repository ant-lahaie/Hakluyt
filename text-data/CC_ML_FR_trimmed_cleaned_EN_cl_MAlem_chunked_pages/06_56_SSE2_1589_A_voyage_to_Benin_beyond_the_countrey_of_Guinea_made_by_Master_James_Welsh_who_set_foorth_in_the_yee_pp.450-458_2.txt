Saturday the first of February 1588 . we be thwart of a round foreland , which i take to be the eastermost part of Capo de tres puntas : and within the say round foreland be a great bay with a island in the say bay . the castle of the second of February we be thwart of the castle Mtna - of Mina , and when the third glass of our lookout be spend , we spy under our larboard - quarter one of their boat with certain negro , and one Portugal in [ ii two . 127 . ] the boat , we will have have he to come aboard , but he will not . and over the castle upon the high rock two white we do see as it may be two watch - house , and they do watch - house . show very white : and we go eastnortheast . the 4 in the morning we be thwart a great high hill , and up into the land be more high ragged hill , monte re - and those i reckon to be but little short of monte dondo .
