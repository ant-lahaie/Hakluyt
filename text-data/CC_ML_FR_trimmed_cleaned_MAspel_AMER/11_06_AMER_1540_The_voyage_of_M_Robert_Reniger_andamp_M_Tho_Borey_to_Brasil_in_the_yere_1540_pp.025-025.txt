And ancient voyage of M. Robert Reniger and M. Thomas Borey to Brasil in the year of our Lord 1540. 
 Have be certainly informed by M. Anthony Garrard and ancient and worshipful merchant of the city of London , that this commodious and gainful voyage to Brasil was ordinarily and usually frequented by M. 
 Robert Reniger , M. Thomas Borey , 
 and diverse other substantial and wealthy merchants of
