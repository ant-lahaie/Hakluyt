the voyage of Swanus one of the son of earl Godwin unto Jerusalem , anno Dom. 1052 , record by William of Malmsburie lib . 2. de gestis regum anglorum , capite 13. 
 the same in english . 
 SWanus be of a perverse disposition , and faithless to the king , often time disagree with his father and his brother Harold : and afterward prove a pirate , he stain the virtue of his ancestor with his robbery upon the sea . last of all , be guilty unto himself of the murder of his kinsman Bruno , and ( as some do report ) of his own brother , he travail unto Jerusalem : and in his return home , be take by the Saracen , be beat , and wound unto death .
