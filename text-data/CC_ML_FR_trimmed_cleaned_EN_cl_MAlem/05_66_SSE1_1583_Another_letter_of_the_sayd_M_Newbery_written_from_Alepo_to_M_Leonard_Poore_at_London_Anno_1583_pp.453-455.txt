God make we thankful for it . the fourteen day of this present we come from Tripoli , and the twenty day arrive here in Aleppo , and with the help of God to morrow or next day , we begin our voyage towards Babylon and Balsara , and so into India . our friend master Barret have he commend to you , who have send you in the Emanuel a ball of nutmeg for the small trifle you send he , which i hope long since you have receive . also he have by his letter certify you in what order he sell those thing , whereof i can say nothing , because i have not see the account thereof , neither have demand it : for ever since our come hither he have be still busy about the dispatch of the ship , and our voyage , and i likewise in buy of thing here to carry to Balsara , and the Indies . we have buy in coral for 1200. and odd ducat , and amber for four hundred ducat , and some soap and break glass , with all other small trifle , all which thing i hope will serve very well for those place that we shall go unto . all the rest of the account of the bark Reinolds be send home in the Emanuel , which be 3600. ducat , which be 200. pound more than it be rate . for master staper rate it but 1100. livre . and it be 1300. pound , so that our part be 200. pound . beside such profit as it shall please God to send thereof : wherefore you shall do very well to speak to m. staper for the account . and if you will content your self to travel for three or four year , i will wish you to come hither or go to Cairo , if any go thither . for we doubt not if you have remain there but three or four month , you will like so well of the place , that i think you will not desire to return again in three or four year .
