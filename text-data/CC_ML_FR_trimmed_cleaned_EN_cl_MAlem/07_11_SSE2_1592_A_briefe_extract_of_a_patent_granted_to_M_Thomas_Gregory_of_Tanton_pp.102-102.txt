a brief extract of a patent grant to m - Thomas Gregory of Tanton , and other , for traffic between the river of Nonnia and the river of Madrabumba and Sierra Leona on the coast of Guinea , in the year 1592. 
 n may the 34 year of our gracious sovereign queen Elizabeth , a patent of speciali licence be grant to Thomas Gregory of Tanton in the county of Somerset , and to Thomas pope , and certain other merchant to traffic into Guinea from the northermost part of 
 the river of Nonnia to the southermost part of the river of Madrabumba and Sierra Leona , and to other part aswell to the southeast as to the northwest , for a certain number of league therein specify which amount to a hundred or thereabouts . which patent be grant for the term of ten year : as appear at large in the say patent record in the roll in her majesty chancery .
