Aguja . it be a cape subject much to flaw , by reason it be a very high land : and within the cape lie a island within 235 the mouth of the sound , which have a white cliff or spot in the westnorthwest part of the island . the land all about the cape rise all in hammock or break steepy hill . a league Southwest within that , ( for so fall the land thereabouts ) there stand on the top of a cliff a watchhouse : and a little within that a small island : you may go in between the main and it , or to leeward if you lust : and hard within that be Santa Martha the road and town of Santa Martha , which at 11 of take . the clock we take , the people all be flee , except a few Spaniard , negro and Indians , , which in a bravado at our land give we some 30 or 40 shot , and so run away . that night their lieutenant general
