Frier Marco de ni ? a depart from saint Michael in the province of culiacan , stand in 24 . degree of northerly latitude : and come to the town of Petatlan , receive many courtesy of the Indians there . depart from thence , he have information of many island , and of a great country inhabit with civil people ; he come to Vacupa : where during his abode , he hear news of Cevola , and of the state of the 7 . city , and of other province , and of the rich island of pearl , which extent northward upon the coast .
