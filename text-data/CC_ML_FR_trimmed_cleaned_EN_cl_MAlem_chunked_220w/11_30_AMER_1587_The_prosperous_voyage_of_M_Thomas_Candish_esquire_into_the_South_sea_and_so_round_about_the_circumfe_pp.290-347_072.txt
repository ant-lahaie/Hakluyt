This be do , the same night we have many firework and more ordinance discharge , to the great admiration of all the Spaniard which be there : for the most part of they have never see the like before . 326 this end , our general discharge the captain , give he a royal reward , with provision for his defence against the Indians and his company , both of sword , target , piece , shoot and powder to his great contentment : but before his departure , he take out of this great ship two young lad bear in japon , which can both wright and read their own language , the old be about 20 year old be name Christopher , the other be call Cosmus , about 17 year of age , both of very good capacity . he take also with he out of their ship
