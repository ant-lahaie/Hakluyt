June . the 1 . day of June our anchor at Isabel , on the north side of Hispaniola . the 3 . day of June , the governor of Isabel , and captain of the port de plata , be certify by the report of sundry Spaniards , who have be well entertained aboard our ship by our general , that in our fleet be many brave and gallant gentleman , who great desire to see the governor aforesaid , he thereupon send gentle commendation to our general , promise within few day to come to he in person , which he perform accord . the 5 . day the aforesaid governor accompany with a lusty friar , and twenty other Spaniard , with their servant , and negro , come down to the sea side , where our ship ride at anchor , who be see , our general man immediate the most part of
