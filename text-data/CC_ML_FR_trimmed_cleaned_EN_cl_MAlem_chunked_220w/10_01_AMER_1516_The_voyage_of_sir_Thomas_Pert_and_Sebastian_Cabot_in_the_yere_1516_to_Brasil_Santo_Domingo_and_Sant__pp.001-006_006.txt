year 1517 . an english rover under the colour of travel to discover , come with a great ship unto the part of Brasill on the coast of the firm land , and from thence he cross over unto this hand of Hispaniola , and arrive near unto the mouth of the haven of this city of saint Domingo , and send his shipboate full of man on shore , and demand leave to enter into this haven , say that he come with merchandise to traffic . but at that very instant the governor of the castle Francis de Tapia cause a tire of ordinance to be shoot from the castle at the ship , for she bear in direct with the haven . when the Englishman see this , they withdraw themselves out , and those that be in the shipboate , get themselves with all speed on shipboard . and in truth the warden of the castle commit a oversight : for if the ship have enter into the haven the man thereof can not have come on land without leave both of the city and of the castle . therefore the people of the ship see how they be receive , sail towards the hand of S .
