the voyage of monsieur Roberval from his fort in Canada unto Saguenay , the five of June , I543 - the 5 . of June . the 6 . of June . monsieur de Royeze . the 14 of June . onsieur Roberval the king lieutenant general in the country of Canada , Saguenay , and Hochelaga , depart towards the say province of Saguenay on the Tuesday the 5 . day of June 1543 . after supper : and he with all his furniture be embark to make the say voyage . but upon a certain occasion they lie in the road over against the place before mention : but on the Wednesday about six of the clock in the morning they set sail , and sail against the stream : in which voyage their whole furniture be of eight bark , aswell great as small , and to the number of threescore and ten person , with the aforesaid general . the general leave behind he in the aforesaid place and fort thirty person to remain there until his return from Saguenay , which he appoint to be the first of July , or else they shall return into France . and he leave there behind he but two bark to carry the say thirty person , and the furniture which be there , while he stay still in the country . and for effectuate hereof , he leave as his lieutenant a gentleman name Monsieur de Royeze , to who he give commission , and charge all man to obey he , and to be at the commandment
