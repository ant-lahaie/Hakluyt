Martine Frobisher , to the northwest , for the search of the straight or passage to China , write by Christopher hall , master in the Gabriel , and make in the year of our lord 1576 . he 7 . of June be Thursday , the two bark , viz . the Gabriel , and the two Michael and our pinnace set sail at Ratcliff , and bear down to Detford , and there we anchor : the cause be , that our pinnace burst her boultsprit , and foremast aboard of a ship that ride at Detford , else we mean to have pass that day by the court then at Grenewich . the 8 . day be Friday , about 12 of the clock we weigh at Detford , and set sail all three of we , and bear down by the court , where we shoot off our ordinance and make the best show we can : her majesty behold the same , commend it , and bid we farewell , with shake her hand at we out of the window . afterwards she send a gentleman aboard of we , who declare that her majesty have good like of our do , and thank we for it , and also will our captain to come the next day to the
