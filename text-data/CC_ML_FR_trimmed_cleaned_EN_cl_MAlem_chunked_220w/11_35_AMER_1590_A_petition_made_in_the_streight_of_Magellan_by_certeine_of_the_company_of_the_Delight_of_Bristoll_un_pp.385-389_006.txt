Thomas Browne answer he present , that he shall send none , for fear least the wind may arise , and by that mean we shall loose 388 so many of our man more : to who Matthew hawlse reply that it be not material , for that he have make choice of a company for the very same purpose , who name be Emmanuel dornel , Richard Hungate , Paul carry , John Davis , Gabriel Valerosa , a Portugal , and Peter a Britain , and the Spaniard which we have take in at the same place , at our first time of water . and thus we end , desire God to send we well into our native country . in witness whereof we have subscribe our name .
