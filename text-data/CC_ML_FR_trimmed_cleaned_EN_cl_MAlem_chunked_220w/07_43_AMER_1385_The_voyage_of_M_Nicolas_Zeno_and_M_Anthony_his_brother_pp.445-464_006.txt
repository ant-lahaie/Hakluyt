Theise sea , forasmuch as they sail , be in manner nothing but shold and rock , in so much that if m . Nicolo and the venetian mariner have not be their pilot , the whole fleet in judgement of all that be in it , have be cast away , so small be the skill of zichmnis man , in respect of we , who have be train up in the art and practice of navigation all the day of their life .
