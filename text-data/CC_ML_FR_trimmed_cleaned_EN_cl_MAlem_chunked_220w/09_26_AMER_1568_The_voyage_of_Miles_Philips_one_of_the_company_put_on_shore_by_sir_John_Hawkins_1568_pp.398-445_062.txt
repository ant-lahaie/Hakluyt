David Alexander and Robert Cooke return to serve the inquisitor , who short after marry they both to two of his negro woman : Richard Williams marry a rich widow of Biskay with 4000 pezo : Paul Horsewell be marry to a Mestisa , as they name those who father be Spaniard , and their mother Indians , and this woman which Paul Horsewell have marry , be say to be the daughter of one that come in with Hernando Cortes the conqueror , who have with she in marriage four thousand pezo , and a fair house : John story be marry to a negro woman : William low have leave and licence to go into Spain where he be now marry : for my own part i can never thorough settle my self to marry in that country , although many fair offer be make unto i of such as be of great ability and wealth , but i can have
