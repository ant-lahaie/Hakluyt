First the dark foggy mist , the continual fall snow and stormy weather which they common be vex with , and now daily ever moor and more increase , have no small argument of the winter draw near . and also the frost every night be so hard congeal within the sound , that if by evil hap they shall be long keep in with contrary wind , it be great to be fear , that they shall be shut up there fast
