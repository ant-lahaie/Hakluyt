Marks of Villa Rica . and by east , for the wind will not suffer we to lie near the east , and one evening at sun go down we fall with a land , that have the same mark to our judgement with the cape de Corrientes : and because night be at hand , we wrought to double cape de Sant Antonio , stir west : and about midnight we have land all high right a head , and the coast lie southwest : and then we cast and lie Northeast till day : and be day , we see the land all a head , and we ply to windward to the east , and keep it a larboard till we have bring it southwest . and to be short , we go here on land in the same place that we first fall with in the evening before : and it be a island call Cozumel , lie on the coast of Yucatan . and this island be the land which we see first , seem by the mark to be
