And be in so good readiness , the general with all the captain come to the court , then lie at Greenwich , to take their leave of her majesty , at who hand they all receive great encouragement , and gracious countenance . her highness beside other good gift , and great promise , bestow on the general a fair chain of gold , and the rest of the captain kiss her hand , take unto 321 ten a hundred man appoint to inhabit there
