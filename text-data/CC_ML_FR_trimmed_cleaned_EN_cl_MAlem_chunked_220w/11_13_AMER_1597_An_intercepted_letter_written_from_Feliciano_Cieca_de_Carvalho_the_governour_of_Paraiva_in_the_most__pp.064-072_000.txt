a speciali letter write from Feliciano Cieza de Carvalsho the governor of Parajua in the most northern part of Brasil , 1597 , to fillip the second king of Spain , answer his desire touch the conquest of Rio Grande , with the relation of the besiege of the castle of Cabodelo by the Frenchman , and of the discovery of a rich silver mine and diverse other important matter . receive your majesty letter bear date the nine of November 1596 . whereby i understand that your majesty do determine to proceed in the discovery and conquest of Rio Grande , accord to the relation which be send your majesty by don Francisco de Sousa , governor general of this realm of Brasilia : together with a copy of a letter , which your majesty send unto we , bear date the two and twenty of march 1596 .
