So come i to the city of Guatimala upon the south sea , which be distant from Mexico about 250 . league , where i stay 6 . day , for that my horse be weak . and from thence i travail still south and by east seven day journey , pass by certain indian town , until i come to a indian town distant from Mexico , direct south 309 . league . and here at this town inquire to go to the port de Cavallos in the north - east sea , it be answer that in travail thither i shall not come to any town in 10 . or 12 . day journey : so here i hire two Indians to be
