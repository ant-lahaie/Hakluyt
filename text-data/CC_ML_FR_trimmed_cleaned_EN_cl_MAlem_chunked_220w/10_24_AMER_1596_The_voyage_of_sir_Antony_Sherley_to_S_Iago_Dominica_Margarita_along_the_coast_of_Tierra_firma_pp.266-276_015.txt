And depart from Jamaica the 6 of march , we sail to cape de Corrientes in Cuba , to look for a bark of m . Parkers for our better strength : but not find she , we go for the cape of Honduras , where we purpose to entrap the watch , and so to sack the town of Truxillo , but the watch discover we , make great fire
