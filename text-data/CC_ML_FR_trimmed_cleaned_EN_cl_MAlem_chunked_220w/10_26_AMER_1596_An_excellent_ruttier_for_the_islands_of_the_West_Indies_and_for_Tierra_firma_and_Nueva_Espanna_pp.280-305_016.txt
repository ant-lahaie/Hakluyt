And be alone , and with a good ship of sail , and draw towards night , than thou must come to anchor behind Morro hermoso : and after the first watch thou must set sail , and go out west and by north , because thou must be sure to keep a seaboord from the island de Arenas , which lie 2 league to seaward right against Samba . and if thou go from cape deal Aguja by night , thou shall go west and by north : and so thou shall go without the force of the water of Rio Grande . and 288 be by day thou shall go along the coast , and shall see Morro hermoso , which , as i have say , lie to the west of Rio Grande , and have for mark , a face of a black land not very high , and it be round . and if thou depart by day from Morro hermoso , thou must go west , and must take heed , as i say before , of the isle de Arenas , which lie north and south of Samba .
