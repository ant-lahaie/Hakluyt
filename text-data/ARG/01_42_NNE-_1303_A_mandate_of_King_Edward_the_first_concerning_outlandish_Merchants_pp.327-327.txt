Mandatum regis Edwardi primi de mercatoribus alienigenis.
MErcatores extranei vendant mercimonia sua in civitate London &c. infra quadraginta dies post ingressum suum, anno 3. Edwardi primi.
The same in English.
A mandate of king Edward the first concerning outlandish marchants.
WE will and command that outlandish marchants doe sei their wares in the citie of London &c. within forty dayes of their arrivall.
