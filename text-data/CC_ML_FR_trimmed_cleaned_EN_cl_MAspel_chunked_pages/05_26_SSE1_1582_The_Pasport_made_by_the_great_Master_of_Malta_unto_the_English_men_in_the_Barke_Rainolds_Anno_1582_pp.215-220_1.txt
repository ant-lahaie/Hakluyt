That every time the said merchants of the said ship , or with any other , shall not bring such merchandise as is forbidden , and that by sufficient proof and letters testimoniali it appears that they are free from the infections of the plague , they may victuali themselves with all necessary victuals , and traffic with us , and in this Hand and dominion , and afterward may depart and follow their voyage whither they will into the Levant or else where , as all other vessels , and especially of France and other nations do , and fell and buy whatsoever merchandise they shall think good . Item , that they may bring powder for cannon and harquebus , saltpeter , coal of Newcastle , plataeae of latin , tin , Steel , iron , common karsies white , course canvas to make sail for the galleys , balls of iron for shot , fine millstones , trees & masts for galleys , little and others , and in conclusion , having seen that they for the time of their abided here , did behave themselves like faithful and catholic Christians , & that his holiness hath allowed the safeconduct of the great Turke to them granted for fear of the Turkish army , and other vessels of the enemy , submitting our selves to the pleasures of his holiness , and especially because our people have occasion to pass by diverse places so far off , as England , Flanders , and all parts Westwards , and in other places , we
