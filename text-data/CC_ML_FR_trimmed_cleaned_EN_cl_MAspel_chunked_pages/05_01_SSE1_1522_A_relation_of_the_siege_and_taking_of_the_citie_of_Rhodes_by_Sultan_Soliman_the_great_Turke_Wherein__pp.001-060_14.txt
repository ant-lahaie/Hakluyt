And if there be any of the principals and worthy men among you that is so disposed , we shall give him wages and provision greater then he hath had . And if any of you will abide in the said isle , ye may so do after your ancient usages and customs , and much better . And therefore if that ye will accept these our oaths and entreatings , send unto us a man with your letters to mine Imperiali majesty , or else know ye that we will come upon you with all provisions of war , and thereof shall come as it pleases God . And this we do , to the end that you may know , and that you may not say , but we have given you warning . And if you do not thus with your good will , we shall vault and undermine your foundations in such manner , that they shall torn upside down , and shall make you slaves , and cause you to die , by the grace of God , as we have done many , and hereof have you no doubt . Written in our court at Constantinople the first day of the month of June . How the Turkes came to land in the Isle of Lango , and were driven to their ships again by the Prior of S . Giles .
