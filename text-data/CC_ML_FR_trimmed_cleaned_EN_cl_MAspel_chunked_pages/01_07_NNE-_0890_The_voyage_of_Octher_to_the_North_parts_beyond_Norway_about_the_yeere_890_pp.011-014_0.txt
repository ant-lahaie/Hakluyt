The voyage of Octher made to the Northeast parts beyond Norway , reported by himself unto Alfred the famous king of England , about the year 890 . Cther said , that the country wherein he dwelled was called Helgoland . Octher told his lord king Alfred that he dwelled furthest North of any other Norman . He said that he dwelled towards the North part of the land towards the West coast : and affirmed that the land , notwithstanding it stretcheth marvelous far towards the North , yet it is all desert and not inhabited , unless it be very few places , here and there , where certain Fins dwell upon the coast , who live by hunting all the Winter , and by fishing in Wynnes live by Summer . He said that upon a certain time he fell into hunting & a fantasy and desire to prove and know how far that land stretched Northward , and whither there were any habitation of men North beyond the desert .
