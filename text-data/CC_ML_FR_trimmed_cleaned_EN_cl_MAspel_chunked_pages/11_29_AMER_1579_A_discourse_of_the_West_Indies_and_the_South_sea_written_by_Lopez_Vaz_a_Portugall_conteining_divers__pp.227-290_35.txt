These Streights The length of ( they say ) extend in length from East to West about the straights of and hundred and twentie leagues . At the middle of these mighty ridg straights on the North side are many mountains covered of mountains . with snow , which mountains stretch from thence along the frontiers of Chili , Peru , and Nuevo reino de Granada , even unto the shore of the North sea at Santa Martha , as I have before signified . It is a wonder to behold the exceeding heighth of these mountains , and the way which they continue covered with snow , for even under the Equinoctial line they have as much snow upon their tops as in 52 degrees . Also it is worthy the remembrance , that all this country toward the South sea is very fruitful , and the people very discreet and gentle : but all the coast towards Brasill upon the North sea is poor , whereas never yet was found any commodity of account , and the people themselves are very cruel and salvage ; for the will of God is , that good and civil men should inhabit fruitful countries .
