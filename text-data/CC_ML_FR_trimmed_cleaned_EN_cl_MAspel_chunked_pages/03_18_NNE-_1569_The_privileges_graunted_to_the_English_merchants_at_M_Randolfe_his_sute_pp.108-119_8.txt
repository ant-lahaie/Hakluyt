And if any Englishman be indebted , we will the Creditor not to cast him in prison , or to deliver him to the Sergeant , lest the officer loose - him , but to take ware in pawn of the debt . 22 Also of our goodness , we have granted the English Merchants to send our Commission to all our Towns , Captains , and authorised men , to defend and guard the said Merchants from all thieves , robbers , and evil disposed persons . 23 If in coming or going to and from our dominions , the Merchants , the factors , or servants be spoiled on the sea , our Counsel shall send our letters , and will them to be sought out , and where they shall find the goods , cause it to be restored again , and the offender to be punished , according to our commandment . 24 Also of our goodness , we have granted the said Merchant ' s to take up Brokers , Packers , Wayers , and such like labourers , as shall be needful for them , paying for their hire as the labourers and they shall agree . 25 We likewise of our goodness , have licensed
