If their wives go out of their houses , as unto the church , or any other place , they go out with great majesty , and with as many men and maids as though she were the wife of some noble man . I will assure you , I have seen a miners wife go to the church with and hundred men , and twenty gentlewomen and maids . They keep open house : who will , may come to eat their meat . They call men with a bell to come to dinner and supper . They are princes in keeping of their houses , and bountiful in all manner of things . Howbeit the same is very costly : for there is never a hundred of quicksilver but costeth at the least threescore pounds sterling .
