Their skins are also very good to make cables for ships , and so used . This kind of whale is much less in quantity then other kinds , having not in length above seven else . And as for the common kind of whale ' s , the place of most and best hunting of them is in his own country : whereof some be 48 . else of length , and some 50 . of which sort he affirmed that he  himself was one of the sixe , which in the space of 3 . days killed threescore . He was a man of exceeding wealth in such riches , wherein the wealth of that country does consist . At the same time that he came to the king , he had of his own breed 600 . tame Dear , of that kind Sixe hundreth which they call Rane Dear : of the which number 6 . were tame eere - stall Rane Dear , a beast of great valve , and marvellously esteemed among the Fynnes , for that with them they catch the wild Rane Dear . He was among the chief men of his country one : and yet he had but 20 . kine , and 20 . swine , and that little which he tilled , he tilled it all with horses .
