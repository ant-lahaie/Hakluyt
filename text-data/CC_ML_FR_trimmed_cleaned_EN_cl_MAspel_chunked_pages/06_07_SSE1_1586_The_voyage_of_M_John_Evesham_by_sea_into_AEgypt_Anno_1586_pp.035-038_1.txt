Also they have very good corn , and very plentiful : all the Country is very hight , especially in the months of August , September , and October . Also within the said City there is a pillar of Marble , called by the Turkes , King Pharaoes needle , & it is foure square , every square is twelve foot , and it is in height 90 foot . Also there is without the walls of the said City , about twentie score paces , another marble pillar , being round , called Pompey his pillar : this pillar stands upon a great square stone , every square is fifteene foot , and the same stone is fifteene foot high , and the compass of the pillar is 37 foot , and the heighth of it is 101 feet , which is a wonder to think how ever it was possible to set the said pillar upon the said square stone . The port of the said City is strongly fortified with two strong Castles , and one other Castle within the city , being all very well planted with munition : and there is to the Eastward of this Cayro .
