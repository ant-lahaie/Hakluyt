You shall need always to have Argos eyes , to spy their secret packing and conveyance , aswell on land as aboard the ship , of and for such furs and other commodities , as yearly they do use to buy , pack and convey hither . If you will be vigilant and secret in this article , you cannot miss to spy their privy packing one with another , either on shore or aboard the ship : work herein wisely , and you shall deserve great thanks of the whole company . io Also at the lading again of the ship , you shall continue and abide aboard , to the end that you may note and write in your book all such goods and merchandises as shall be brought and laden , which you shall orderly note in all sorts as heretofore , as in the second article partly it is touched : and in any wise put the Master and the company in remembrance , to look and foresee substantially to the roomaging of the ship ,
