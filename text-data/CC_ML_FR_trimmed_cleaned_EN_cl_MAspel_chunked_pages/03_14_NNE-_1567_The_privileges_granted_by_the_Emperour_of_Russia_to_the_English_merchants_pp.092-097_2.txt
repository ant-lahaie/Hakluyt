And likewise we have granted them to buy and fell in all our kingdoms and castles , with all kind of wares : and we have also licenced them , that when those English merchant ' s do desire to buy & fell with our merchant ' s wholly together , that they shall have liberty so to do wholly together : and they that do desire to fell their own wares by retail in their own house , that then they fell it in their own house by retail to our people & other strangers , as they can agree : and weights and arshnids to be kept in their house with our seal , and they themselves to barter and fell their own wares : and no Russe merchant in Mosco , or any other place in our kingdom to fell for them any wares , nor to buy or barter any wares for them , nor colour any strangers goods . And whereas those merchant ' s of England , sir William Garrard and his company do desire to fell their wares at Colmogro , Dwina , Vologda , Yeraslave , Costrum , and in Novogrod the lower , Cazan , Astracan , great Novgorod , Plesko , Narve , Dorpe , and in other our towns & castles , they shall have their will to fell it : and of their wares of England and Russeland no custom shall taken , neither they nor their wares shall stayed in any place : and when they depart out of Mosco , to advertise our chancellor thereof , and not to give any note or inventory of any kind of their wares they carry away : and when the English merchant ' s , sir William Garrard and his company
