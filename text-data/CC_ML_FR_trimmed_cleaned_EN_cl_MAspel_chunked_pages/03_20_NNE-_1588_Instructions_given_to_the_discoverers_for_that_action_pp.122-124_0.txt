Nicholas , or any part of Dwina Reca ) you shall from thence , even as timely in the spring as the ice will permit you , sail , & make all expedition that may be , unto the mouth of the river Pechora ( as your commission does lead you ) and as you pass by the coast all alongst ( notwithstanding the plate that shows you the description of the said coast , from Dwina unto Vaigats ) yet you shall seek by all the means that you can , to amend the same plate , using as many observations , as you possibly can do : and these notes following are to be observed by you principally . 1 First , that you do observe the latitude as often , and in as many places as you may possibly do it , noting diligently the place where you do so observe the same . 2 Also that you do diligently set with your compass , how the land does lie from point to point , all alongst 122 as you go , and to use your judgements how far there may be between each of them . 3 Item , that you do always use to draw the proportion and biting of the land , aswell the lying out of the points , and headlands , unto the which you shall give some apt names ( at your discretion ) as also the form of the Baize , and to make some mark in drawing the form , and border of the same , where the high cliffs are , and where low land is , whither sandy hills , or whatsoever : omit not to note any thing that may be sensible and apparent to you , which may serve to any purpose . 4 In passing along by any coast , that you keep your led going often times , and found at the least once every glass , and
