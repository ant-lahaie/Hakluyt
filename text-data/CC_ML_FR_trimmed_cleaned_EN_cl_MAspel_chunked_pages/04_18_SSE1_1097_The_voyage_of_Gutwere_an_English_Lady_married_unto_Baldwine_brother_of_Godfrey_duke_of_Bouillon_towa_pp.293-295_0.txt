As reporteth William Archbishop of Tyre , lib . 3 . cap . 17 . hist , belli sacri . The same author in the 10 . book 8c first chapter of the same historic concerning the same English Lady , writeth further as follows , Baldwine having followed the wars for a time , gave his mind to marriage , so that being in England he fell in love with a very honourable and noble Lady nam Gutwere , whom he married and carried with him in that first happy expedition , wherein he accompanied his brothers the Lords , duke Godfrey and Eustace , persons very commendable in all virtues and of immortali memory . But he had hard fortune in his journey , because his foresaid wife , being wearied with a long sickness finished her life with a happy end near the city of Marasia , before the Christian army came unto Antioch , where she was honourably buried , as we have declared before . Chronicon Hierosolymitanum in lib . 3 . cap . 27 . makes also mention of this English Lady , which he calls Godwera in this manner .
