This island hath much plain ground in it in many places , and many fair and straight trees do grow upon it , fit for to make excellent good masts for all sorts of ships . There are also mines of very fine gold in it which are in the custody of the Indians . And to the Southward of this place , there is another very great island , which is not subdued by the Spaniards , nor any other nation .
