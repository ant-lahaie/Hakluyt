To whom the ancient patriarch answered , that he was content : whereupon that cursed Jewe brought him a cup of the most venomous and deadly poison that could be found , which the holy Patriarch having perceived , said : In the name of the father , of the son , and of the holy Ghost . : and having so said he drank it quite up ; which done , he took a drop of pure water , putting it into that very cup , and gave it unto the Jewe , saying unto him , I in the name of my Christ have drunk thy poison , and therefore in the name of thy expected Messias , drink this water of mine within thine own cup . Whereupon the Jewe 339 took the cup out of the hand of the Patriarch , and having drunk the water , within half and hour burst a sunder . And the Patriarch had none other hurt , save that he became somewhat pale in sighed , and so remained ever after . And this miracle ( which merits to be called no less ) was done to the great commendation of the holy Patriarch in the presence of a thousand persons , and namely of the Soldan of Egypt : who seeing the despite of the Jewes , unto their own cost and confusion compelled them to make the conduct , which with so many engines comes into the castle from Nilus above mentioned . And this triumphant Patriarch not long since was alive , and in perfect health , which God continue long time . [ IL i . 203 . ] Of the preparation of the Carovan to go to Mecca .
