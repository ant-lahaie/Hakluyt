At length turning his tale to another matter , he began to quarrel with king Richard , pretending as though he sought causes to 326 i 191 . break with him , and to malign him : and therefore he forged ( said he ) these lies upon him , and all because he by that means would avoid to marry with Alise his sister , according as he had promised . Adding moreover that if he would so do , and would not marry the said Alise his sister according to his oath , he would be and enemy to him , and to his , while he lived . To this king Richard said again , that he could by no means marry that woman , forsomuch as his father had carnail copulation with her , and also had by her a son : for proof whereof he had there presently to bring forth diverse & sundry witnesses to the kings face , to testify with him . In conclusion , through counsel and persuasion of diverse about the French king , agreement at last was made , so that king Philip did acquit king Richard from this bound of marrying his sister , and king Richard again should be bound to pay to him every year for the space of five years , two thousand marks , with certain other conditions beside , not greatly materiali for this place . And thus peace being between them concluded the 28 day of the said month of March , the [ II i . 24 . ] French king launching out of the haven of Messana , the 22 day after in the Easter week , came with his army to the siege of Achon .
