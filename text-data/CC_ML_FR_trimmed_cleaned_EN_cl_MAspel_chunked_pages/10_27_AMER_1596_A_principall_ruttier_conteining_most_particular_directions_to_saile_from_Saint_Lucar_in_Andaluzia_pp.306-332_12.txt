IF you will seek the river called Rio Hermoso , looking well within the land , you shall see three hammocks of and high hill , and those two which are to the landward within , are rounder then the other which is nearest the sea , for that it is longer and bigger , and lies North and South , and you shall be 4 . leagues at sea when you shall see them : and they are called The sierras , or mountains of Tamaclipa ; and from thence to the river of Panuco there is no high land , but all low and even with the sea , and full of palm trees and other trees .
