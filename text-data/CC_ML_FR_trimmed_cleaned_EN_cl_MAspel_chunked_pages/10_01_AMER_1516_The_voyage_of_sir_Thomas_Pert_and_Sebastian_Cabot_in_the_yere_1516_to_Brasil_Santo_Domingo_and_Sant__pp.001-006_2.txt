And in mine opinion it will not seem well to leave so great and profitable and enterprise , seeing it may so easily and with so little cost , labour and danger be followed and obtained . Though hitherto your grace have made thereof a proof , & found not the commodity thereby as you trusted , at this time it shall none impediment : for there may be now provided remedies for things then lacked , and the inconveniences and lets removed , that then were cause your graces desire took no full effect : which is , the courses to be changed , and to follow the aforesaid new courses . And concerning the mariners , ships , and provision , and order may be devised and taken meet and convenient , much better then hitherto : by reason whereof , & by Gods grace , no doubt your purpose shall take effect .
