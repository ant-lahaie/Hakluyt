Now Godred and his company rising early in the morning , and seeing themselves beset with their enemies on all sides , they were utterly astonished . Howbeit arming themselves they began stoutly to make resistance , but altogether in vain . For about 9 . of the clock in the morning , Olavus and the foresaid viscount Pol , with all their soldiers , entered the Island , and having slain all whom they found without the precincts of the Church , they apprehended Godred , gelding him , and putting out his eyes . Unto which actium Olavus gave not his consent , neither could he withstand it , by reason of the forenamed viscount the son of Book . This was done in the year of Christ 1223 . The next summer following Olavus having received pledges from all the chief men of the Islands , with a fleet of 32 . ships sailed unto Man , and arrived at Rognolfwaht . At the same time Reginald and Olavus divided the kingdom of the Islands between themselves , Man being granted unto Reginald , & beside his portion the name of a king also . Olavus having received certain victuals of the people of Man , returned , together with his company , unto his own portion of Islands . The year following Reginald taking unto him Alanus lord of Galway , together with his subject ' s of Man , sailed unto the Islands , that he might take away that portion of ground from his brother Olavus , which he had granted unto him , and subdue it unto himself . How - 1228 . , beit , by reason that the people of Man had no list to fight against Olavus or the Islanders , because they bore good will towards them , Reginald and Alanus lord of Galway being defeated of their purpose , returned home unto their own .
