London to have be a City of great traffic rum lib . 2 . and Merchandise not long after the beginning of the Saxons reign . tium . The same in English . IN the year of the incarnation of Christ 604 . Augustine Archbishop of Britaine consecrated two Bishops , to wit Mellitus and Justus . He appointed Mellitus to preach to the East Saxons , which are divided from Kent by the river of Thames , and border upon the Eastern sea , whose chief and Metropolitan City is London , seated upon the bank of the aforesaid river , which is also a Marte Town of many nations , which repair thither by sea and by land .
