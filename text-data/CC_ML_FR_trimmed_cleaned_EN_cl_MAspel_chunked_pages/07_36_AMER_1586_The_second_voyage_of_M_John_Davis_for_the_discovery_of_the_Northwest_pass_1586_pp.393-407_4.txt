These people are very simple in all their conversation , Great thieves . but marvelous thievish , especially for iron , which they have in great account . They began through our lenity to show their vile nature : they began to cut our cables : they cut away the Moonelights boat from her stern , they cut our cloth where it lay to air , though we did carefully look unto it , they stole our oars , a caliver , a boar spear , a sword , with diverse other things
