And forasmuch as the entering in is so dangerous , according as I have certified your Majesty , there can no ship come into this harbour , but we must needs sink them ; so that these defences shall not only be annoyance to the enemy , but also animate and encourage the inhabitants of this city : for they have been and are in such fear of the enemy , and pirates , that if we had not made these fortifications , strengthened the city in this order , and put some soldiers in garrison , the citizens would have fled , and forsaken this city : for all the persuasions made to them by the governor could not persuade them to the contrary , but they would be gone , if it had not been for this fortification , and yet for all this we have much to do to make them to stay here : so now by reason of these soldiers which shall come hither , the people of
