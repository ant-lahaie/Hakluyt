These Indians being of colour tawny like and Olive , having every one of them both men and women , hair all black , and no other colour , the women wearing the same hanging down to their shoulders , and the men rounded , and without beards , neither men nor women suffering any hair to grow in any part of their body , but daily pull it off as it grows . They go all naked , the men covering no part of their body but their yard , upon the which they wear a gourd or piece of cane , made fast with a thrid about their loins , leaving the other parts of their members uncovered , whereof they take no shame . The women also are uncovered , saving with a cloth which they wear a hand - breath , wherewith they cover their privities both before and behind .
