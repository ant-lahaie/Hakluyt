Item , in the foresaid year , and about the feast aforesaid , the forenamed Godekins and Stertebeker , with other their accomplices , unjustly took upon the sea a certain ship of Thomas Lyderpole , and John Coote of Wiveton : and the master and mariners which were in the said ship , they villanously slew , among whom they put to death one Simon Andrew , the godson , nephew , and servant of the foresaid Simon Durham . Which ship , with the goods and furniture that were therein was worth 410 . nobles . Wiveton . Item , in the very same year , about the feast aforesaid , the forenamed Godekins and Stertebeker & other their accomplices wickedly spoiled a certain ship of the foresaid Simon Durham called the Dogger , wherein Gervase Cat was master , lying at and anchor , while the company were occupied about fishing , and likewise unjustly took away with them the salt fish , and furniture of the said ship . Moreover , the master and his company that were in the said Dogger they beat and wounded , so that they utterly lost their fishing for that year , the master and his said company being endamaged thereby , to the sum of 200 . nobles . Wiveton . Item , in the year of our Lord 1396 . the foresaid Godekins and Stertebeker , and other their accomplices unjustly took upon the sea a certain crayer , called the Buss of Zeland , which one John Ligate merchant , and servant unto the forenamed Simon Durham had laden in Prussia , on the behalf of the said Simon , to sail for England , and spoiled the said craier , and also took and carried away with them the goods and merchandises 66 of the said Simon , being in the foresaid ship , to the valve of 66 . pounds .
