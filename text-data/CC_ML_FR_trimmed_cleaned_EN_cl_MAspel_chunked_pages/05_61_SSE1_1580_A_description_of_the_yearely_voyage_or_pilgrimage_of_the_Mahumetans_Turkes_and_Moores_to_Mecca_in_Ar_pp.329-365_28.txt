This ceremony being ended , the pilgrimss enter into the Mosquita , and drawing near unto the house of Abraham , they go round about it other seven times , always saying : This is the house of God , and of his servant Abraham ; This done , they go to kiss that black stone abovesaid . After they go unto the pond Zun Zun , and in their apparel as they be , they wash [ II i . 209 . ] themselves from head to foot , saying , Tobah Allah , Tobah Allah , that is to say , Pardon lord , Pardon lord , drinking also of that water , which is both muddy , filthy , and of and ill savour , and in this wise washed and watered , every one returns to his place of abided , and these ceremonies every one is bound to do once at the least . But those which have a mind to overgo their fellows , and to go into paradise before the rest , do the same once a day while the Carovan remains there .
