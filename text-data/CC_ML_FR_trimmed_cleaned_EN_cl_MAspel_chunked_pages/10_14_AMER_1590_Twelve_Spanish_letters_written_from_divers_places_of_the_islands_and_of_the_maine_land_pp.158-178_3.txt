Our Lord keep your Majesty many years . From Havana the 20 of October , 1590 . Your Majesties servant , whose royal feet I kiss , John de Trexeda governor of Havana . A letter sent to DONE Petro de Xibar one of his Majesties privy Counsel of the West Indies , from DONE Diego Mendez de Valdes Governor of S . Juan de Puerto Rico the 20 of November 1590 , touching the state of that City and Island . Received your honours letter the 20 of Februarie , whereby I received great content , to hear that your honour is in good health . As touching the imprisonment of our cousin DONE Pedro de Valdes , it doth grieve me to the very soul . I beseech God to send him his liberty : and likewise the imprisonment of Diego Flores de Valdes grieveth me very much : I pray God to send good justice . The M . of the field Juan de Texela , and the M . workman Juan Baptista Antonio arrived here in safety , and have viewed this City with all the circuit round about and the situation as I have informed his majesty thereof . They have marked a place to built a strong Fort , whereat the country remains very well content .
