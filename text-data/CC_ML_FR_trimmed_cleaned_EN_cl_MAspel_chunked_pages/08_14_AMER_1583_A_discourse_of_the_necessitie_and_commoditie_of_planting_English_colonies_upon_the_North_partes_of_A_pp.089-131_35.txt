Moreover , things in the like journeys of greatest price and cost as victuali ( whereof there is great plenty to be had in that country without money ) and powder , great artillery , or corselets are not needful in so plentiful and chargeable manner , as the show of such a journey may present : for a small quantity of all these , to furnish the Fort only , will suffice until such time as diverse commodities may be found out in those parts , which may be thought well worthy a greater charge .
