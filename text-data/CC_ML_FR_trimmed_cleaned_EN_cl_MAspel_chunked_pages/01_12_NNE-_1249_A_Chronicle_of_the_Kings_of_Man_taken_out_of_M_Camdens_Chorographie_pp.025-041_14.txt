Also certain pirates coming to the south part of Man , wasted & spoiled it . The monks of Russin conveyed the body of K . Reginald , unto the abbey of S . Marry of Furnace , & there he was interred in the place , which his own self had chosen for the purpose . After these things Olavus traveled unto the king of Norway , but before he was arrived there , Haco king of Norway appointed acertaine noble man nam Husbac the son of Owmund , to be king of the Islands of the Hebrides & called his name Haco . Then came the said Haco with Olavus & Godred DONE the son of Reginald , and a multitude of Norwegians , unto the islands : and while they were giving and assault unto a castle in the island of Both , Haco being hit with a stone died , and was buried in Iona . In the year 1230 . came Olavus , with Godredus DONE , & certain Norwegians unto Man , and they parted the kingdom among themselves , Olavus still retaining Man . Godred as he was going unto the islands , was slain in the isle of Lewis , & Olavus enjoyed the kingdom of the islands also . In the year 1237 . upon the 12 . of the Kalends of June , Olavus son of Godred king of Man deceased in the isle of S . Patric , and was interred in the abbey of Russin . He reigned 11 . years , two while his brother was alive , and nine after his death . Haraldus his son being of the age of 14 . years , succeeded , and he reigned 12 . years .
