There was also abundant store of victuals , salt , and all kind of provision for shipping and the war : which was confessed by the said Commissary of victuals taken there , to be the beginning of a magasin of all sorts of provision for a new voyage into England : whereby you may conjecture what the spoil thereof hath advantaged us , and prejudiced the king of Spaine . The next morning about eight of the clock the enemies abandoned their ships . And having overcharged the artillery of the gallion , left her on fire , which burned in terrible sort two days together , the fire and overcharging of the pieces being so great , as of fifty that were in her , there were not above sixteene taken out whole ; the rest with overcharge of the powder being broken , and melted with heat of the fire , were taken out in broken pieces into diverse ships . The same day was the cloister on the South side of the town entered by us , which joined very near to the wall of the town , 486 out of the chambers and other places whereof we beatrice into the same with our musketeers .
