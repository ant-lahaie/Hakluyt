Then he blesses the water which is in the pot , and does breathe over it : then he takes all the candles which the gosseps have , and holding them all in one hand lets part of them drop into the water , and then gives every one his candle again , and when the water is sanctified , he takes the child and holds it in a small tub , and one of the Godfathers takes the pot with warm water , and poureth it all upon the child ' s head .
