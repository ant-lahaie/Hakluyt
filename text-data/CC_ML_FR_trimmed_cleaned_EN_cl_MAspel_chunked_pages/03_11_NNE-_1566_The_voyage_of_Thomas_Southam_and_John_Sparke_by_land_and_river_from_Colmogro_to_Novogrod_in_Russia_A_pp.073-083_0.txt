The way discovered by water by us Thomas Southam & John Spark , from the town of Colmogro , by the Western bottom of the Bay of S . Nicholas , unto the city of Novgorod in Russia , containing many particulars of the way , and distance of miles , as hereafter followeth . Anno 1566 . E departed from Colmogro about 10 . of the clock afore noon in a Lodia or Bark , which we hired to bring us along the coast to a place called Soroka , & in the said bark we hired 6 . mariners , and a boy to conduct us to the place before rehearsed . The Lodia or bark was of the burden of 25 . tons or thereabouts , wherewith we valed down the river of Dwina , the wind being then calm , unto a monastery called S . Michael where we were constrained to anchor because of a contrary wind which there met us .
