And that your self also will without fail , upon the terms appointed for the said payments , procure satisfaction to be made accordingly unto our endamaged subject ' s with the sums due unto them by reason of their losses , whereof they have sufficient information . Wherefore in regard of those your friendly letters , and your courteous answer returned by them unto us , as is aforesaid , we do yield unto you right unfeigned thanks .
