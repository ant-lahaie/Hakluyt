Then did he bring me to the water tower of the same City , that by a sleight and devise hath the water brought up as high , as any Church in the town , and to tell you the strange devises of all , it passes my capacity . Then he brought me to another fair garden , called the Shooters hoose , where are buts for the long bow , the cross bow , the stone bow , the long piece , and for diverse other exercises more . After this , we walked about the walls of the City , where is a great , broad , and deep ditch , upon one side of the town , so full of fish , as ever I saw any pond in my life , and it is reserved only for the States of the City . And upon the other side of the City is also a deep place all green , wherein Dear are kept , and when it pleases the States to hunt for their pleasure , thither they resort , and have their courses with grayhounds , which are kept for that purpose . The fift of May , I departed from Augusta towards Venice . Venice , and came thither upon Whitsunday , the thirteenth of the same month .
