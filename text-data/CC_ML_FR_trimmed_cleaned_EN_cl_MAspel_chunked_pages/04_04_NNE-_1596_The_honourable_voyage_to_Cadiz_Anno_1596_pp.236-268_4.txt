His nostris religiosis petitionibus concede , Domine , sacrosanctam & annuentem voluntatem tuam . After that we had anchored at Plymmouth that night , as I have said , the third of June very early in the morning , having a reasonable fresh gale of wind , we set sail , and kept our course again , and the ninth of the same month coming something near to the North cape , in a manner in the same altitude , or not much differing , which was about xliii . degrees , and something more , yet bearing so , as it was impossible to be descried from the land : There it pleased the Lords to call a select Council , which was always done by hanging out of a flag of the arms of England , and shooting ofF of a great warning piece . Of this select or privy Council , were no more then these : The two Lords general , 240 the Lord Thomas Howard , the Lord Warden Sir Walter Raleigh , the Lord Martiali Sir Francis Vere , Sir George Carry master of the Ordinance , Sir Coniers Clifford , and Sir Anthony Ashley , Clarke of the said Counsel .
