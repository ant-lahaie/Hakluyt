the voyage of John Lacy to Jerusalem . Nno domini 1172 fundata fuit abbatia de Stanlaw per dominum johannem Lacy constabularium cestriae and dominum de Halton , qui obiit in terra sancta anno sequenti : qui fuit vicessimus annus regni regis henrici secundi . the same in english . in the year of our lord 1172 be found the abbey of Stanlaw by the lord John Lacy constable of Chester , and lord of Halton , who decease in the holy land the year follow : which be in the twenty year of king Henry the second .
