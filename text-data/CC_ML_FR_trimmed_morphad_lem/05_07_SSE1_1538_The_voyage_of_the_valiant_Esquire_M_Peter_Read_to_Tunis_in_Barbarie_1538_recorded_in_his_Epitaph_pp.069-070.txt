the epitaph of the valiant esquire m. Peter [ ii i . 99 ] Read in the south he of saint Peter church in the city of Norwich , which be knight by Charles the lift at the win of Tunis in the year of our lord 1538. here under lie the corpse of Peter read esquire , who have worthy serve , not only his prince and country , but also the emperor Charles the five , 69 both at his conquest of Barbary , and at his siege at Tunis , as also in other place . who have give he by the say emperor for his valiant deed the order of Barbary . who die the 29 day of December , in the year of our lord God 1566.
