Dato in 266 Algieri in nostro reggio Palazzo , sigillato del nostro reggio sigillo , e fermato della grand ferma , and scritto del nostro reggio Secretario , ill the 23 de Jenaro , 1583. the same in english . we Assan Bassha viceroy and lieutenant , and captain of the jurisdiction of Algiers , give and grant free safeconduct to Thomas Shingleton merchant , that with his ship and mariner , of what nation soever they be , and with his merchandise of what country soever , he may go and come , and trade and traffic free in this city of Algiers , and other place of our jurisdiction , as well of the west as of the east . and in like sort we further command the captain of the sea of Algiers , and other place of our jurisdiction , the reiz of vessel and captain of the Levant , and other captain of vessel aswell great as small , whosoever they be , we do command they , that find the foresay Thomas Shingleton Englishman in the sea of Genua , France , Naples , Calabria , and Sardinia , with his ship and merchandise , [ ii i . 175. ] and man of what nation soever they be , that they molest they not , neither take nor touch any kind of thing of they , neither money nor any other kind of good , under pain and peril of loose of their life and good : and as you make account of the favour of the grand signior our lord sultan murate hottoman , so see you let he pass on his way without any manner of impediment . Dated at Alger in our kingly palace , sign with our princely signet , and seal with our great seal , and write by our secretary of estate , the 23. of Januarie , 1583.
