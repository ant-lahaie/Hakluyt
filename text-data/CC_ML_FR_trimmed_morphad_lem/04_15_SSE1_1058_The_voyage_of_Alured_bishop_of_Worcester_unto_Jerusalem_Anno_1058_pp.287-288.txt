the voyage of Alured bishop of Worcester unto Jerusalem , an . 1058. record by Roger Hoveden in part priore annalium , folio 255. linea 15. Luredus Wigorniensis Episcopus eccle - A.D. 1058. siam , quam in civitate Glavorna a fundamentis construxerat , in honore principis Apostolorum Petri honorifice dedicavit : and postea regis licentia wol-stanum Wigorniensem monachus a se ordinatum abbatem constituit ibidem . dein praesulatu dimisso Wiltoniensis ecclesiae , qui sibi ad regendum commissus fuerat , and Hermanno , cujus supra mentionem fecimus , reddito , mare transiit , and per Hungariam profectus est hierosolymam , etc the same in english . in the year of our lord 1058. Alured bishop of Worcester , very solemn dedicate a church ( which himself have found and build in the city of Gloucester ) 287 unto the honour of saint Peter the chief apostle : and afterward by the king permission ordain Wolstan a monk of Worcester of his own choice , to be Abbate in the same place . and then have leave his bishopric which be commit unto he over the church of Wilton , and have resign the same unto Hermannus above mention , pass over the sea , and travail through Hungary unto Jerusalem , etc
