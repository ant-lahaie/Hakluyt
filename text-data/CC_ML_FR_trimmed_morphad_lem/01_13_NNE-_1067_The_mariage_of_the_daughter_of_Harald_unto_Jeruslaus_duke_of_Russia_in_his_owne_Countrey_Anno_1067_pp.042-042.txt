the marriage of the daughter of Harald , slay by William the conqueror , unto Jeruslaus duke of Russia , take out of the 9 book of the danish history write by saxon Grammaticus . An. D. 1067. 1067. Araldo caeso , filii ejus dvo confestim in daniam cum sorore migrarunt . quos Sweno , paterni illorum meriti oblitus consanguineae pietatis more accepit , puellamque Ruthenorum regi Waldemaro , ( qui and ipse Jarislaus a suis est appellatus ) nuptum dedit . Eidem postmodum nostri temporis dux , ut sanguinis , ita and nominis haeres , ex filia nepos obvenit . itaque hinc Britannicus , inde eous sanguis in salutarem nostri principis ortum confluens communem stirpem duarum gentium ornamentum effecit . the same in english . HArald be slay his two son with their sister speed themselves immediate into Denmark . who Sweno forget their father desert receive in most kind and friendly manner , and bestow the young damosel in marriage upon Waldemarus king of Russia who be also call by his subject Jarislaus . afterwards the say Waldemarus have by his daughter a nephew be duke at this present , who succeed his predecessor both in lineal descent and in name also . wherefore the english blood on the one side and the russian on the other side concur to the joyful birth of our prince , cause that mutual kindred to be a ornament unto both nation .
