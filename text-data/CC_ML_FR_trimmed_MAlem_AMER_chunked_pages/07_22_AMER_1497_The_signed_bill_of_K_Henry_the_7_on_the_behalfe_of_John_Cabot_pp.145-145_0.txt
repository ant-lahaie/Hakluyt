billa signata anno 13 henrici septimi . ex tertio die Februarii , anno 13 , licentiam dedit Joanni caboto , quod ipse capere possit sex nave Anglicanas , in aliquo portu , sive portibus regni angliae , ita quod sint de portagio 200 . doliorum , vel subtus , cum apparatu requisito , and quod recipere possit in dictas nave omnes tale magistros , marinario , and subdito regis , qui cum eo exire voluerint , etc a record of the roll touch the voyage of John Cabot and Sebastian his son . the same in english . [ Iii . 6 . ] the king upon the three day of February , in the 13 year of his reign , give licence to John Cabot to take six english ship in any haven or haven of the realm of England , be of the burden of 200 ton , or under , with all necessary furniture , and to take also into the say ship all such master , mariner , and subject of the king as willing will go with he , etc
