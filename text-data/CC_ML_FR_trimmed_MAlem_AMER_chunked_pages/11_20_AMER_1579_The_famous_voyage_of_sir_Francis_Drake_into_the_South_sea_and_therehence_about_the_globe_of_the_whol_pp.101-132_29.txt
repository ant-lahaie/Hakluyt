Theise canoas be furnish with warlike munition , every man for the most part have his sword and target , with his dagger , beside other weapon , as lance , caliver , dart , bow and arrow : also every Canoa have a small cast base mount at the least one full yard upon a stock set upright . thus come near our ship , in order they row about we , one after another , and pass by , do their homage with great solemnity , the great personage begin with great gravity and fatherly countenance , signify that the king have send they to conduct our ship into a better road . soon after the king himself repair , accompany with 6 . grave and ancient person , who do their obeisance with marvellous humility . the king be a man of tall stature , and seem to be much delight with the sound of our music , to who as also to his nobility , our general give present , wherewith they be pass well content . at length the king crave leave of our general to depart , promise the next day to come aboard , and in the mean time to send we such victual , as be necessary for our provision : so that the same night we receive of they meal , which they call Sagu , make of the top of certain tree , taste in the mouth like sour
