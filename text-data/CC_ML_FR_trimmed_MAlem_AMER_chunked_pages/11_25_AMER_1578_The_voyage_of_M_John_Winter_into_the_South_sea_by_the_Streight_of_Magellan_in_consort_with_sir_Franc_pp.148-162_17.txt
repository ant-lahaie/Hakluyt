Francis Drake , and have be drive with he down to the southerly latitude of 57 degree , and afterward pass back by the same strait again , it please God to bring we safe into our own native country to enjoy the presence of our dear friend and kinsfolk , to who be praise , honour , and glory , for ever and ever . amen . Edward cliff , mariner .
