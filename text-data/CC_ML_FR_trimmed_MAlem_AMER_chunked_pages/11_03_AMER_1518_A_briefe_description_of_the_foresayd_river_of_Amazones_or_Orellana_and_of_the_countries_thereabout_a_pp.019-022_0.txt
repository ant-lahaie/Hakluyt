Arannon lie in seven degree and a half northward of the equinoctial , it be a great river , and have more than fifteen league in breadth eight league within the land . it have many island , and in this river within the land forty league there be near to the say river a mountain , whereupon grow tree of incense , the tree be of a good height , and the bough thereof be like to plumtree , and the incense do hang at they , as the ice do at the tile of a house in the winter season when it do freeze . in this river be take four Indian in a small boat , call in the Indian language a Canoa , that come down by the river , and there be take from they two stone of emerald , the one of they be as great as a man hand . they say that so many day journey go upward by the river , they find a rock of that stone . likewise there be take from they two loaf make of flower , which be like to cake of soap , and it seem that 19 A .
