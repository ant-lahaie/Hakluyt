So the next morning we go to seek out the Frenchman pinnace : which be at Laguna we shoot off a piece , and so she come to we , have in her three moor of 199 they wrack upon Bermuda the 17 of December 1593 . the english voyage our company , Edmund Barker our lieutenant , and one John west , and Richard Lucland one of the mutinous crew . the which i tell the Frenchman off ; and he can not deny , but that there be such a thing pretend . than i be put into the french pinnace to seek their boat :
