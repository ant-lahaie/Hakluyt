Theise ox be of the bigness and colour of our bull , but their home be not so great . they have a great bunch upon their fore shoulder , and more hair on their fore part then on their hinder part : and it be like wool . they have as it be a horse - mane upon their back bone , and much hair and very long from the knee downward . they have great tuffe of hair hang down their forehead , and it seem that they have beard , because of the great store of hair hang down at their chin and throat . the male have very long tail , and a great knob or flock at the end : so that in some respect they resemble the lion , and in some other the Carnell . they push with their home , they run , they overtake and kill a horse when they be in their rage and anger . final , it be a foul and fierce beast of countenance and form of body . the horse flee from they , either because of their deform shape , or else because they have never see they . their master have no other riches nor substance : of they they eat , they drink , they apparel , they shoe themselves : and of their hide they make many thing , as house , shoe , apparel and rope : of their bone they make bodkin : of their sinew and hair , thread : off their home , maw , and bladder , vessel : of their dung , fire : and of their calves - skinne , budget , wherein they draw and keep water . to be short , they make so many thing of they as they have need of , or as many as suffice they in the use of this life . there be also in this country other beast as big as horse , which because they have home and fine wool , they call they sheep , and they say that every home of they weigh be fifty pound weight . there be also great dog which will fight with a bull , and will carry fifty pound weight in sack when they 168 go on hunt , or when they remove from place to place with their flock and herd .
