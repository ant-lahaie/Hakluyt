 we will and command all baliff of Portes , at the which the cog of Norway ( wherein certain of the king of Norwaie his soldier , and certain merchant of Saxony be come for England ) shall touch , that , when the foresay cog shall chance to arrive at any of their haven , they do permit the say cog safe to remain in their say haven so long as need shall require , and without impediment also free to depart thence , whensoever the governor of the say ship shall think it expedient . witness the king .
