the voyage of Ranulph earl of Chester , of Saer Quincy earl of Winchester , William de Albany earl of Arundel , with diverse other noble man to the holy land , in the second year of king Henry the three . Matth. Paris . holensh . page . 202. 
 n the year 1218 , Ranulph earl of Chester be send into the holy land by king Henry the three with a goodly company of soldier and man of war , to aid the Christians there against the infidel , which at the same time have besiege the city of Damiata in Egypt . 
 in which enterprise the valiancy of the same earl after his come thither be to his great praise most apparent . there go with he in that journey Saer de Quincy earl of Winchester , William de Albany earl of Arundel , beside diverse baron , as the lord Robert fitz Walter , John constable of Chester , William de Harecourt , and Oliver fitz roy son to the king of England , and diverse other .
