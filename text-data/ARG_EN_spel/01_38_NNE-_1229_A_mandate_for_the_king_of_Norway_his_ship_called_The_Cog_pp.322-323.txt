A Mandate for the King of Norway his Ship called the Cog. 
 we will and command all baliffs of Portes , at the which the Cog of Norway ( wherein certain of the king of Norwaie his soldiers , and certain Merchants of Saxonie are coming for England ) shall touch , that , when the foresaid Cog shall chance to arrive at any of their Havens , they do permit the said Cog safely to remain in their said Havens so long as need shall require , and without impediment also freely to depart thence , whensoever the governors of the said ship shall think it expedient .
