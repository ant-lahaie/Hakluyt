A great supply of money to the Holy land by Henry the 2. 
 A.D. 
 1177. 
 [ II i . 18. ] 
 He same year King Henry the second being at Waltham , assigned and aid to the maintenance of the Christian soldiers in the Holy land , That is 
 to wit , two and fortie thousand marks of silver , and five 
 hundred marks of gold . Matth. Paris , and Helens .
