the three voyage set forth by sir Walter Ralegh to Guiana , with a pinnace call the Watte , in the year 1596 . write by m . Thomas Mash be a gentleman of the company . Pon Thursday the 14 . of October 1596 . we set sail from limehouse upon the river of Thames , and through much contrariety of wind and other accident , we make it the 27 . of December , before we can get out of Waimouth . the 25 . of Januarie in the morning we come to the north side of the island of grand Canaria , where we hope to have get a boat to serve we upon the coast of Guiana
