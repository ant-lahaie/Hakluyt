Canoa aboard we , and one of the chief Portugal that belong to the place . here we take in beef , hog , water and wood at our pleasure , have almost no man able to resist we , but some of our Portugal steal from we in the Canoa . the 11 day we enter into the haven of Baya , where we be receive at the point come in , with two great piece of ordinance , which discharge bullet at we five time a piece , but they lose shot and powder , and do we not harm . after we have pass the point , we hail in for the road as close as the wind
