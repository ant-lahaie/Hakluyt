a extract of a letter of captain Francis Vasques de Coronado , write to a secretary of the right noble don Antonio de Mendosa , viceroy of Nueva Espanna . Dated in culiacan , the 8 . of march 1539 he say that Frier Marcus de Ni $ a arrive in the province of Topira , where he find all the Indians flee unto the mountain for fear of the christian , and that for his sake they come all down to see he , with great joy and boldness . they be man of good make , and white than other , and their woman be more beautiful than other of the neighbourprovince . there be no great city there , yet
