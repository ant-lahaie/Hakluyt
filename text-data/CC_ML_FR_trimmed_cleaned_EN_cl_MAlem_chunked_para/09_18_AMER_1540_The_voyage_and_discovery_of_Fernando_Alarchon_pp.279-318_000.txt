the relation of the navigation and discovery which captain Fernando Alarchon make by the order of the right honourable lord don Antonio de Mendosa viceroy of new Spain , date in Colima , a haven of new Spain . Chap . 1 . Fernando Alarchon after he have suffer a storm , arrive with his fleet at the haven of saint Iago , and from thence at the haven of Aguaiaval : he be in great peril in seek to discover a bay , and get out of the same he discover a river on the coast with a great current , enter into the same , and coast along he descry a great many of Indians with their weapon :
