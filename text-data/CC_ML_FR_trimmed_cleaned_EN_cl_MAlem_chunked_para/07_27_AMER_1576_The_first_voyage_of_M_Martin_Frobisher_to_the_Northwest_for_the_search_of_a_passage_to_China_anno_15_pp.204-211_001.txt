day be Friday , about 12 of the clock we weigh at Detford , and set sail all three of we , and bear down by the court , where we shoot off our ordinance and make the best show we can : her majesty behold the same , commend it , and bid we farewell , with shake her hand at we out of the window . afterwards she send a gentleman aboard of we , who declare that her majesty have good like of our do , and thank we for it , and also will our captain to come the next day to the court to take his leave of she . the same day towards night m . secretary Woolly
