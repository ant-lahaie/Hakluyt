15 sail of good ship , whereof the whole number be to return again with their load of gold over in the end of the summer , except those 3 ship , which shall be leave for the use of those captain which shall inhabit there the whole year . And be in so good readiness , the general with all the captain come to the court , then lie at Greenwich , to take their leave of her majesty , at who hand they all receive great encouragement , and gracious countenance . her highness beside other good gift , and great promise , bestow on the general a fair chain of gold , and the rest of the captain
