so soon as we be come to a anchor in Totnes ride under mount Raleigh , we espy four white four white bear at the foot of the mount : we suppose they to eares - be goat or wolf , man our boat and go towards they : but when we come near the shore , we find they to be white bear of a monstrous bigness : we be desirous of fresh victuali and the sport , begin to assault they , and i be on land , one of they come down the hill right against i : my piece be charge with hailshot and a bullet : i discharge my piece and shoot he in the neck ;
