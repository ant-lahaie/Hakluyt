Waterin be a river forty league distant northward from saint Helena , where any fleet of great ship may ride safe . i take this river to be that which we call waren in Virginia , whither at Christmas last 1585 . the Spaniard send a bark with forty man to discover where we be seat : in which bark be Nicholas Burgoignon the reporter
