and before night they meet with a ship of Panama , which they present board , but take nothing from she but only a negro , and so leave it , hold on their course . the next day be the first of February , they meet another ship that sail to Panama , lade with fish and other victual , and forty bar of silver , and some gold , but i know not how much , which they take , and send the passenger ( with two friar that be in she ) in a boat to land . the next day they hang a man of the ship , because he will not confess two plate of gold
