Domingo to go for Nueva Espanna . i advise thou that if thou will go from Santo Domingo for Nueva Espanna , thou shall go Southwest and by south , and so thou shall have sight of Punta de Nizao , which be a low point , and be the end of the hill call Sierras de las mina viejas , and towards the northwest of they thou shall see a low land , and to go into Hocoa thou shall stir from this point of Nizao westnorthwest , and thou shall see the point of Puerto Hermoso , and the bay that it make : and thou must be sure to keep near the shore to find a good road
