Antonio Zeni two gentleman of Venice which live in those part . out of which collection i do add concern the description of Estotiland aforesaid these particular follow . Estotiland ( say he ) abound with all thing necessary for mankind . in the mid thereof stand a exceed high mountain , from which issue four river that moisten all the country . the inhabitant be witty and most expert in all mechanical art . they have a kind of peculiar language and letter . howbeit in this king library be preserve certain latin book , which they understand not , be perhaps leave there not many year before by some Europeans , which traffic thither . they have all kind of metal
