this realm of Brasilia : together with a copy of a letter , which your majesty send unto we , bear date the two and twenty of march 1596 . Moreover i receive another letter from your majesty bear date the 15 of march 1597 . both which letter be to one effect . it may please your majesty to understand that there be diverse gentleman in these country of as good ability as my self , which seek to live at home only for their ease and pleasure , and be not wont to hazard nor venture their body , life , and good so often time in your majesty service as i have do and common do ;
