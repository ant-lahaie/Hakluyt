Puna , where be a very good harbour , where we find a great ship of the burden of 250 ton ride at a anchor with all her furniture , which be ready to be hale on ground : for there be a speciali good place for that purpose . we sink it , and go on shore where the lord of the hand dwell , which be by the water side , who have a sumptuous house marvellous well contrive with very many singular good room and chamber in it : and out of every chamber be frame a gallery with a stately prospect into the sea on the one side , and into the hand on the other
