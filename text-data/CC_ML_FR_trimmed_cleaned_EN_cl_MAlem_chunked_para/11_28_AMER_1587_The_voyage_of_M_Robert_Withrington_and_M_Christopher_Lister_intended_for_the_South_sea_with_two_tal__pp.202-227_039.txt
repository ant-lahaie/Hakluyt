the next day be the 8 of February , there fall out many and diverse speech on each part concern the alter of our course , some will continue for the strait , and other some will not . whereupon a view be take in both ship of victual , and reasonable store be find for both company : and the wind withal come to the north , we determine to take out of the prize the best necessary that be in they , and so cast they off , and to ply for the strait . all this time we hold on our course , and the 15 day we find our self in the height of 44 degree ,
