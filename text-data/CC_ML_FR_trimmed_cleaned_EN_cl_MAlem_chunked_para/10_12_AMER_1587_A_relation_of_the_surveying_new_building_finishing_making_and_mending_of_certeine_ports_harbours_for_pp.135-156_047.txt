Panama . PAnama be the principali city of this diocese : it lie 18 . league from number de dios on the south sea , and stand in 9 degree . there be 3 . monastery in this say city of friar , the one be of dominick , the other be of augustines , and the three be of saint Francis friar : also there be a college of Jesuit , and the royal audience or chancery
