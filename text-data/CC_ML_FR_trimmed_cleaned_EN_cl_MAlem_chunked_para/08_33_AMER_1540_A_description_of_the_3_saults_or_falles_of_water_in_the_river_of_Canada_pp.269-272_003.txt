7 . of Septem . the town oftutonaguy . bid ground and a great current . a town of friendly people . another village of good people which dwell over against the second salt . and leave they with he to learn their language , and bestow upon he a cloak of Paris read , which cloak be set with yellow and white button of tin , and small bell . and withal he give he two basin of Laton , and certain hachet and knife : whereat the say lord seem high to rejoice , and thank the captain . this do , the captain and his company depart from that place : and we sail with so prosperous a wind , that we arrive the eleven day
