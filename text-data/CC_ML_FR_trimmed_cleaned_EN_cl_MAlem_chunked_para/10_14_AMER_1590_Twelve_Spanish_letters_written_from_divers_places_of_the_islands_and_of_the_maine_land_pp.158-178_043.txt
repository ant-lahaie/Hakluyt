in Peru . he last year 1588 i receive letter from your worship and from my sister : and since that time i have receive none , nor in the fleet which come to Cartagena 1589 . and this year 1590 there have famine . bene great want of corn in this kingdom of Potossi : for that there have be no rain in this kingdom of long time . for in march the husbandman use to sow their corn : and in April winter do begin . and if in April there be no rain , the corn which be sow will consume away : and so for want of rain we have have two bid year of corn
