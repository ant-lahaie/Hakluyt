Edward Fenton be appoint general : write by m . luke ward his viceadmiral , and captain of the Edward Bonaventure , begin anno Dom . 1582 . he second of April i depart with the Edward Bonaventure from Blackwall , and the 19 of the same arrive in Nettle ride at Hampton , where i find ride the Gallion Leicester : and so remain there till the first of May , we set sail thence in the forenoon , be of we in the whole fleet four sail . 1 the Gallion Leicester of 400 ton admiral , whereof be general captain Edward Fenton , and William Hawkins the young lieutenant
