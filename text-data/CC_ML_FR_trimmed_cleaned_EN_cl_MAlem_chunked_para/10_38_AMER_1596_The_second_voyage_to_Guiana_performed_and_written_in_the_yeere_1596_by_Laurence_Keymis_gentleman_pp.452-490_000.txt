the second voyage to Guiana . Unday the 26 . of January , in the year of our lord 1596 . we depart from Portland road , in the darling of London , have in company the discoverer , a small pinnace , who we lose at sea , in foul weather , the Thursday next follow . Friday , the 13 . of February , we fall with the Canarie island , where we expect our pinnace , accord to our appointment , seven or eight day . here we take two boat , the one a passenger , we bulge , the other we tow at our ship stern , steer southsouthwest for the island of cape Verde .
