Theise Tartars ( they say ) be a most barbarous kind of people , of a brown colour , with long hair on their head and beard like the moscovite : they live by hunt and fish , and neglect husbandry . this northerly princedom of Zuegara ( as the say governor report to our father ) abound with excellent grape both black and white , which without the labour of man grow natural in all place , and he promise the father to bring some of these vine to Miac6 , that they may be plant there .
