Letters take at sea by captain George Popham . j594 - Alonso his letter from the grand Canaria to his brother be commander of saint Lucar , concern el Dorado . there have be certain letter receive here of late , of a land new discover call Nuevo Dorado , from the son of certain inhabitant of this city , who be in the discovery : they write of wonderful riches to be find in the say Dorado , and that gold there be in great abundance : the course to fall with it be fifty league to the windward of Margarita .
