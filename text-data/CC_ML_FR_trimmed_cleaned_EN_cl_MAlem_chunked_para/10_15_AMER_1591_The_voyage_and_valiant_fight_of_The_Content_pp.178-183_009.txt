but we so gall they with our musket , that we put they from our quarter . than they wind their galley , come up into our stern , and with the way that the galley have , do so violent thrust in the board of our captain cabin , that her nose come into it , mind to give we all their prow , and so to sink we . but we be resolute , so ply they with our small shot , that they can have no time to discharge their great ordinance : and when they begin to approach , we heave into they a ball of fire , and by that mean put they off : whereupon they once again fall astern
