THomas Chaloner be by birth a londiner , by study a cantabrigian , by education a courtier , by religion a devout and true christian . therefore after he have confirm his youth and mind in the study of good learning , when sir Henry Knevet be send ambassador from the mighty prince Henry the 8. to the emperor Charles the five , he go with he as his familiar friend , or as one of his council . at which time the say Charles the 5. pass over from Genoa and Corsica to Alger in Africa in warlike sort , with a mighty army by sea , that honourable Knevet the king ambassador , Thomas Chaloner , Henry Knolles , and Henry Isham , right worthy person , of their own accord accompany he in that expedition , and serve he in that war , wherein Thomas Chaloner escape most wonderful with his life . for the gay wherein he be , be either dash against the rock , or shake with mighty storm , and so cast away , after he have save himself a long while by swim , when his strength fail he , his arm and hand be faint and weary , with great difficulty lay hold with his tooth on a cable , which be cast out of the next gay , not without break and loss of certain of his tooth , at length recover himself , and return home into his country in safety . the voyage of m.
