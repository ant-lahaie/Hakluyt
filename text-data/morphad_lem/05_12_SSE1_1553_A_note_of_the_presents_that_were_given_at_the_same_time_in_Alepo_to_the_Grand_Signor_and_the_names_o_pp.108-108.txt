So the whole army of the grand signior , contain as well those that go by the mountain , as also those that come to Aleppo in company with he , with horseman and footman , and the conductor of the camel and victual , be the number of 300000. man . the camel which carry munition and victual for the say army , be in number 200000. a note of the present that be give at the same time in Aleppo , to the grand signior , and the name of the presenter . First the bassa of Aleppo , which be as a viceroy , present 100 garment of cloth of gold , and 25. horse . the bassa of Damascus , present 100 garment of cloth of gold , and twenty horse , with diverse sort of comfit , in great quantity . the bassa of Aman present 100 garment of cloth of gold , 20. horse , and a cup of gold , with two thousand ducat . the saniaque of Tripoli present six camel , charge all with silk , 20. horse , and a little clock of gold , garnish with precious stone , esteem worth two hundred ducat .
