The voyage of John Lacy to Jerusalem.



Nno Domini 1172 fundata fuit abbatia de Stanlaw per dominum Johannem Lacy Constabularium Cestriae & dominum de Halton, qui obiit in Terra sancta anno sequenti: qui fuit vicessimus annus



regni regis Henrici secundi.



The same in English.



IN the yere of our Lord 1172 was founded the abbey of Stanlaw by the lord John Lacy Constable of Chester, & lord of Halton, who deceased in the Holy land the yere following : which was in the twentieth yere of king Henry the second.
