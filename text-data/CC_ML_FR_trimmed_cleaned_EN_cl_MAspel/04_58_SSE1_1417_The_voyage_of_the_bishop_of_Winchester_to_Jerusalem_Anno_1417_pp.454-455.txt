The Voyage of the bishop of Winchester to Jerusalem , in the sixt year of the reign of Henry the fift , which was the year of our Lord , 1417. Thomas Walsing . 
 454 
 THe last day of October the bishop of Winchester came to the Council of Constance , which after the choosing of the Pope determined to take his journey to Jerusalem : where his eloquent persuasion so much prevailed , that he both persuaded my lords the Cardinals to unity and concord , and also moved them to proceed more speedily to the election of the Pope .
